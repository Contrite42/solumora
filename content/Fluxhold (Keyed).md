## Notes

Fluxhold (Keyed) functions identically to [[Fluxhold]] except that its stored [[Flux]] is locked to a specific paired mark. Only a caster bearing the corresponding [[Sigils|sigil]] mark can draw from it — anyone else who attempts to tap the reservoir finds it inert.

This makes it the standard personal reservoir carried by working [[Flux Users|Formulists]], field casters, and anyone who needs a reliable private Flux supply. The pairing mark is typically inscribed at the same time as the reservoir rune, during the same session.

Like all Fluxhold variants, the rune burns out entirely when depleted and must be re-inscribed. The pairing mark on the caster remains intact after burnout and can be re-paired to a fresh rune.
