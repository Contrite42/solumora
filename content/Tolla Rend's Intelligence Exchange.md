# Tolla Rend's Intelligence Exchange

Tolla Rend is an independent information broker operating from [[Solhaven]], occasionally visible in [[Ashford]], and rumored to maintain safe houses throughout the border region. She is approximately 50 years old, [[Control Tier|T4]], and has been collecting and trading information across the [[Terravelle]]-[[Auralis]] boundary for over twenty-five years.

Her operation is not licensed by either kingdom. It operates because Tolla Rend's information is reliable enough that official agents on both sides quietly maintain relationships with her. She does not spy for governments. She sells information to whoever pays, and she maintains her credibility by ensuring that what she sells is accurate and that her clients' purchases do not become known unless the client wants them known.

## Information Profile

Tolla specializes in:
- **Movement patterns** — who is traveling the border routes, when, and with what cargo.
- **Personnel changes** — new administrators, garrison shifts, promotion patterns in either kingdom's frontier institutions.
- **Economic disruptions** — trade flows that have changed, merchant houses that are struggling, markets that are opening.
- **Voice and tone shifts** — subtle changes in how official correspondence is worded, which suggests institutional pressure or realignment.
- **Crew composition** — who is hiring expedition personnel, what skills they are asking for, what equipment ratios suggest about planned routes or objectives.

She does not collect state secrets or classified military information. Such work draws the wrong kind of attention and the profit margins are poor compared to the operational risk. Instead, she collects the information that half-official people need to make decisions: Is the trade route going to stay open? Is that administrative change political or logistical? How many people is [[Auralis]] actually recruiting for desert expeditions this season?

## How It Works

Tolla maintains a network of 30-40 part-time informants distributed across [[Ashford]], [[Halveth]], [[Valdenmoor]], and [[Solhaven]]. They are couriers, dock workers, merchant house employees, administrative staff, guards, and stable operators — people who see movement and conversation but whose access to classified information is zero. They observe and report. Tolla synthesizes patterns and sells interpretations.

Clients include merchant houses planning trade strategy, expedition companies assessing risk and opportunity, independent practitioners seeking information on which cities are hiring or which research paths are likely to yield institutional support, and occasional official agents from both kingdoms who need to know something that official channels will not reliably tell them.

Payment is handled through merchants houses that accept anonymous deposits. Tolla's reputation is her guarantee — if her information proves incorrect, word spreads through the small network of people who can afford to pay for proprietary intelligence, and her business evaporates. This creates powerful incentive toward accuracy.

## Constraints and Advantages

Tolla is not a spy. She does not have access to confidential documents or encrypted communications. She cannot tell you what [[Davan Rhyce]] is planning or what [[Auralis]]' strategic intentions are toward [[Terravelle]]. She can tell you that certain trade documentation requirements have shifted, that Auralis border garrison rotations have accelerated, and that the types of questions the Council is asking about eastern [[Terravelle]] have changed in ways that suggest preparatory intelligence gathering.

This makes her information less classified but more useful to people making decisions in the present moment. A merchant considering expansion into border trade wants to know whether institutional policy is shifting. A expedition company wants to understand garrison staffing changes. A practitioner wants to know whether cities are in growth phase or contraction phase. Tolla sells these things.
