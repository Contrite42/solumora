**Vend Aln** is twenty-four years old and completed his Luminant training in [[Solhaven]] eighteen months ago. [[Control Tier|T3]]. He is assigned to a Path chapter in [[Solhaven]]'s middle terraces, where his responsibilities are the standard junior Luminant work: ceremony assistance, record maintenance, the pastoral visits that senior Luminants schedule but do not always have time to conduct personally, and the preparatory interviews for tier assessment ceremonies that the chapter administers.

He chose the Path at sixteen after a period he would describe, if asked, as genuine conviction. The conviction is still present. It has become more complicated than it was at sixteen, which he did not expect and which the training did not fully prepare him for.

The complication is not theological. It is observational. He has now conducted fourteen pastoral visits to T0 individuals in the chapter's district and has delivered the consolation framework twelve times. He believes the framework is true in its important claims. He also believes that delivering it accurately and delivering it sufficiently are two different things, and that the gap between them is something he cannot close with more careful delivery. The framework is what it is. The gap is structural.

He has not raised this observation with any of the senior Luminants. The junior Luminant who raises structural critiques eighteen months into their appointment is not necessarily wrong, but is raising the question in the wrong way at the wrong time with the wrong amount of accumulated standing to make the question productive. He understands this. He is keeping the observation.

He has started supplementing the standard pastoral visits with follow-up contact — informal, not officially recorded — that maintains ongoing connection rather than concluding at the ceremony. He has not been told not to do this.
