Keld Orun is a [[Control Tier|T9]] conduit living under self-imposed isolation on the outer administrative fringe of [[Wolfpoint]], where he maintains one monitored workshop and avoids all discretionary public casting.

He is 51 years old. Most people in his district know only that he is a hazardous specialist with unusual restrictions. Very few know his assessed ceiling.

## Capability and Risk

Keld's raw capacity is not socially usable in ordinary settings. At T9, minor precision drift can produce long-lived environmental consequences that outlast any immediate objective. This is why he does almost no active work despite rare-demand requests from institutions in both kingdoms.

He accepts only two classes of tasks:

- containment review when existing anomalies threaten to propagate  
- advisory modeling for high-tier failure prevention in controlled research proposals

He does not execute offensive work, political demonstration casts, or private patron commissions regardless of compensation.

## Adaptation Strategy

Keld's life is organized around non-use and auditability.

- All active casting requires pre-registered intention notes.  
- Sessions are observed by independent technical witnesses.  
- Abort conditions are declared before any channeling begins.  
- Post-session environmental checks run for days, not hours.

Most weeks, he casts nothing above routine personal maintenance. He spends the majority of time writing failure models and reviewing other teams' plans for structural impossibilities that mid-tier planners may miss.

## Institutional Relationship

[[Wolfpoint]] tolerates Keld through practical containment agreements rather than cultural comfort. He contributes to local safety planning, accepts oversight, and remains geographically and procedurally bounded. In exchange, the settlement avoids politicizing his presence and refuses outside pressure to convert him into strategic leverage.

Both [[Terravelle]] and [[Auralis]] have attempted indirect recruitment through research intermediaries. Keld's standing response is unchanged: he will advise on prevention, not perform high-tier power projection for state competition.

## Social Cost

Isolation is not symbolic. It is logistical.

Keld does not attend crowded civic events. He avoids ad hoc travel. He maintains minimal personal network and keeps communication mostly written, partly to preserve clear records and partly to avoid being pulled into urgency-driven requests that bypass safeguards.

People sometimes describe this as austerity. It is closer to damage minimization.

## Why He Matters

Keld embodies the least romantic truth about the top of the tier scale: at T9, responsible practice often means exercising restraint so extreme that from the outside it looks like absence.

His contribution is not what he casts. It is what does not break because he refuses to cast casually.
