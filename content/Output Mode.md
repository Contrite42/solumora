[[Output Mode]] defines the _payload_ of a spell—the type of energy or interaction that actually manifests when [[Flux]] is shaped into effect. If [[Discipline]] is the method/interface, Output Mode is what the world experiences.

When Output Mode is not specified by the spell’s [[Shape]], it defaults to Raw.

## Output Modes (Scholar Standard)

- Raw  
   Unrefined Flux output. Produces blunt, generic effects with low efficiency and limited nuance, but is easy to reproduce.
  ![[Output__RAW 1.png]]
- Thermal  
   Heat transfer and temperature change. Used for warming, burning, chilling, thermal sealing, and phase-change pressure effects.
  ![[Output__THERMAL 1.png]]
- Kinetic  
   Physical force and motion. Used for pushes, impacts, lifting, pressure waves, and sustained movement when paired with Move + Control.
  ![[Output__KINETIC 1.png]]
- Shock  
   Electrical discharge and rapid disruption. Used for stunning, arcing, ignition triggers, and interference with mechanisms.
  ![[Output__SHOCK 1.png]]
- Reactive  
   Chemical interaction output. Used for corrosion, neutralization, smoke/fog production, bonding/adhesion, and controlled decomposition.
  ![[Output__REACTIVE 1.png]]
- Photonic  
   Light emission and optical interaction. Used for illumination, glare, projection, concealment, and visual distortion.
  ![[Output__PHOTONIC 1.png]]
- Sonic
  Vibrational output. Used for silence breaks, resonance, concussive bursts, communication, and disorientation.
  ![[Output__SONIC 2.png]]
- Neuro
  Nervous-system interaction. Used for pain response, numbness, reflex disruption, attention capture, and sensory “overrides” (often paired with Mind [[Discipline]]).
  ![[Output__NEURO 1.png]]
- Constraint  
   Restriction and lock behavior. Used for binding, holding, suppressing, halting motion, limiting flow, and “hard rules” in Wards.
  ![[Output__CONSTRAINT 1.png]]
- Soul  
   Identity-signature interaction. Used for marking, oath-binding, severance, restoration, and effects that key off “true identity” rather than physical form.
  ![[Output__SOUL 1.png]]

## Output Mode vs [[Discipline]]

- [[Output Mode]] = _what manifests_ (payload).
- [[Discipline]] = _how it is shaped/applied_ (interface + rules).

Example: Binding + Thermal produces “thermal rules” (heat can’t cross, heat is pinned, heat is redirected), while Heat + Thermal is straightforward temperature manipulation.

## Practical notes

- Output Modes with clear physical behavior (Thermal, Kinetic, Photonic, Sonic) are common in mass grimoires.
- Output Modes that touch biology or identity (Neuro, Soul) are usually restricted or require higher [[Control Tier]] and stricter [[Target Spec]] definitions.
  _See also: [[Discipline]], [[Hook]], [[Spell Variables]], [[Sigils]]_
