The Solhaven Elevation Works Office is a municipal institution that maintains terrace integrity, vertical transit corridors, and load-bearing Flux infrastructure in [[Solhaven]].

When the city feels seamless, it is because this office did its job.

## Funding

Funding comes from terrace property levies, harbor throughput allocations, and direct infrastructure appropriations from [[The Council of Auralis]].

## Recruitment

The office recruits structural practitioners, survey teams, and inspection clerks from across [[Auralis]], with advancement tied to safety record and incident response speed.

## Rules

- no new upper-terrace load without inspection sign-off
- emergency closures override private access rights
- maintenance crews log all active sigil changes before shift end

## Opposition

The office is regularly pressured by private patrons seeking priority access and by unlicensed contractors who cut safety steps to win faster bids.

## What It Trades In

It trades in permits, maintenance priority, and confidence that the city does not fail under its own weight.
