## Notes

Fluxhold is the standard reservoir rune used across [[Solumora]]. It inscribes a Flux-holding constraint onto a physical object — a stone, a metal disc, a carved token — which then passively draws in and stores ambient [[Flux]] over time. The charge rate depends on the ambient Flux density of the environment and the size of the inscribed object. Larger surfaces hold more; richer environments charge faster.

The rune holds until its stored Flux is fully depleted, at which point the sigil burns out and the object must be re-inscribed. Fluxhold does not discriminate — any caster can draw from it, which makes it useful for shared workspaces and communal tools but a liability if left unattended.

For keyed or conditional variants see [[Fluxhold (Keyed)]] and [[Fluxhold (Conditional)]].
