The Hedun Quiet Contract Office is a legal brokerage institution in [[Hedun]] for sensitive service agreements that clients do not want processed through standard public channels.

It does not make work legal. It makes legal work discreet.

## Funding

The office runs on placement fees, confidentiality retainers, and arbitration commissions tied to completed contracts.

## Recruitment

Recruitment favors experienced scribes, mediators, and handlers with proven discretion records in Hedun's hospitality and maritime sectors.

## Rules

- identity escrow required for all parties
- no contract accepted without defined dispute path
- violent terms are rejected and reported to city authority

## Opposition

The office is disliked by hardline moralists and by black-market brokers who lose business when clients choose enforceable private contracts instead of informal coercion.

## What It Trades In

It trades in deniability, compliance, and usable proof if a contract fails.
