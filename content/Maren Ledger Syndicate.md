The Maren Ledger Syndicate is a Terravellian freight-accounting institution that clears contracts across the [[Maren Freight Chain]] before volume reaches [[Ashford]] export docks.

It exists because delayed cargo is expensive, but disputed cargo is worse.

## Funding

The Syndicate takes a small clearing fee on validated manifests and receives standing retainers from larger [[Trade Guilds]] chapters in [[Valdenmoor]] and Ashford.

## Recruitment

Most staff are mid-tier clerks and dispatch scribes who already worked river contracts. Senior desks are usually filled by former assessor-track members from guild documentation offices.

## Rules

- no release without a matched ledger entry in [[Watts]]
- no route reassignment after lock without two-signature authorization
- all exception handling logged for later arbitration

## Opposition

The Syndicate's main enemies are unofficial brokers who profit from ambiguity and anyone moving unauthenticated grimoires through freight channels tied to [[Black Market Grimoire Circulation]].

## What It Trades In

It trades in timing confidence, route credit, and document trust.
