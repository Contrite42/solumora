_(Council Member, Auralis — Expansion Faction)_

**Yara Venn** is thirty-one, the youngest member of the Expansion Faction, and the person [[Castor Helme]] is currently developing as a future voice. [[Tovan Ashce]] is watching her for the sloppiness. Both of them are correct about what they see.

## Character

Yara arrived at the council two years ago with the particular energy of someone who has been moving fast their whole life and has never encountered a wall that didn't eventually give. She is quick, persuasive, and occasionally imprecise — not from ignorance, but from the tendency to reach the right conclusion quickly and then not check whether the steps between were solid.

She is good at the performative parts of council work: the rooms, the relationships, the kind of social maneuvering that reads as effortless from the outside. She is still learning the slower parts — the patience required for institutional work, the difference between moving a conversation and moving an outcome. [[Castor Helme|Castor]] is teaching her the first. He is not teaching her the second because he does not fully understand it himself.

Her [[Flux]] ability is T4, Chemical discipline, which she uses with the particular enthusiasm of someone who is still discovering what it can do. She has been disciplined in not using it in council chambers since [[Castor Helme|Castor]] told her it was not done, and the discipline has held, which is a meaningful fact about her.

## The Sloppiness

The sloppiness [[Tovan Ashce|Tovan]] is watching for is not incompetence. Yara is not incompetent. It is the pattern of confident action based on incomplete information, followed by adjustment when the information arrives, with no apparent recognition that the adjustment represents an error. It is a pattern that is manageable when the stakes are low and consequential when they are not.

She has twice advocated for positions in full council that turned out to be based on incomplete intelligence, once on trade route conditions and once on something more sensitive involving border community sentiment. Both times she pivoted cleanly when the fuller picture emerged. Neither time did she appear to update the underlying habit.

Tovan has not said anything to her. He is waiting to see if she gets there herself.
