A **Hook** is the core action kernel of a spell — the verb that tells [[Flux]] what kind of work it is doing. While [[Discipline]] and [[Output Mode]] describe _how_ Flux is shaped and _what_ it manifests as, the Hook determines **what the spell is trying to accomplish**.

Hooks are written at the **center** of a sigil and are treated as the spell's primary identity. Most "spell names" in common speech are shorthand for a particular Hook + a typical set of variables.

---

## Hook List (Scholar Standard)

**Emit**
Releases an effect into reality. Used for bursts, projections, and "make a thing happen now."
![[Hook__EMIT.png]]

---

**Shape**
Forms, sculpts, or stabilizes an effect. Used to refine geometry, smooth control, or convert rough output into a clean pattern.
![[Hook__SHAPE.png]]

---

**Bind**
Attaches an effect to something or locks a rule in place. Used for restraints, tethers, adhesion, and "stick this behavior to that target."
![[Hook__BIND.png]]

---

**Ward**
Creates a standing boundary rule. Used for barriers, zones of permission/denial, defensive circles, and persistent protections.
![[Hook__WARD.png]]

---

**Trigger**
Waits for a condition, then fires. Used for traps, delayed releases, thresholds, and conditional activation.
![[Hook__TRIGGER.png]]

---

**Transform**
Changes the state of a target. Used for heating/cooling, hardening/softening, mending/breaking, converting one condition into another.
![[Hook__TRANSFORM.png]]

---

**Move**
Repositions or controls motion. Used for lift/drag/push/pull, guided movement, and sustained telekinetic control.
![[Hook__MOVE.png]]

---

**Sense**
Detects or measures. Used to locate, identify, track, or "listen" for Flux signatures or physical conditions.
![[Hook__SENSE.png]]

---

**Filter**
Selects, excludes, or gates. Used to refine what a spell affects (only metal, only marked, only hostile, etc.) and to enforce constraints.
![[Hook__FILTER.png]]

---

**Amplify**
Increases intensity or efficiency. Used to boost magnitude, extend reach, harden a ward, or enhance a spell's output within safe limits.
![[Hook__AMPLIFY.png]]

---

**Dampen**
Decreases intensity or efficiency. Used to lower magnitude, restrict reach, weaken a ward, or dampen a spell's output.
![[Hook__DAMPEN.png]]

---

**Counter**
Intercepts and neutralizes an incoming [[Flux]] effect before it resolves. Used to cancel spells, disrupt Flux feeds mid-cast, and block incoming effects.
![[Hook__COUNTER.png]]

---

## Relationship to [[Mode]]

A Hook describes _what kind_ of action is happening, while [[Mode]] describes _how_ that action operates:

- **Create** — generates the effect from Flux
- **Affect** — alters something that already exists
- **Control** — steers/maintains an ongoing effect

**Example:** Move + Control is sustained levitation/telekinesis, while Move + Create is a generated push impulse.
