**Solen Carr** is eighteen years old and was assessed [[Control Tier|T5]] six months ago, in a regional assessment held in [[Greyveil]] that he attended expecting to come back with a T2 or T3 result and help his family plan the next few years accordingly.

The assessment assessor asked him to repeat two of the exercises. He repeated them. The assessor left the room. When she came back she brought a second assessor with her. After the second assessor observed the exercises, both of them left the room together. When they came back the result was T5, and the conversation that followed was about things that had not been part of any conversation he had prepared for.

He is from a farming settlement two days north of Greyveil. His parents assessed T1 and T2 respectively and have farmed the same land for twenty years. He has two siblings, both assessed in the working range, both in the early years of trades that fit the community they grew up in. None of this prepared him for T5, and none of the people around him had frameworks for T5 that weren't theoretical.

In the six months since the assessment, he has received formal correspondence from two scholarship institutions in [[Valdenmoor]], a letter from a military recruitment officer in [[Ashford]], and an informal message, routed through the Greyveil guild office, from someone affiliated with [[Wolfpoint]] who said the settlement welcomed practitioners who wanted to develop their capability in an environment organized around serious work.

He has not responded to any of them. He has been helping his family with the harvest, which needed doing, and thinking about what he actually wants, which he does not yet know.

The T5 means he is capable of things he has not learned to do. He has been living with this fact for six months. It is large in a way he does not yet have language for and has not tried to explain to anyone who was not in that assessment room.
