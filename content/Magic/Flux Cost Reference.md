The precision demands of [[Flux Expenditure]] follow predictable patterns based on [[Discipline]] baseline costs, [[Shape]] multipliers, and [[Spell Variables|variable]] specifications. This reference provides approximate tier requirements for common combinations.

## Discipline Base Costs

**Physical Disciplines (Low Baseline)**
- **Force**: Baseline +0 tiers. The cheapest discipline to channel.
- **Heat**: Baseline +0.5 tiers. Nearly as efficient as Force.

**Physical-Complex Disciplines (Moderate Baseline)**
- **Electric**: Baseline +1 tier. Requires precise pathway control.
- **Chemical**: Baseline +1 tier. Molecular interaction demands refinement.
- **Sound**: Baseline +1 tier. Frequency control adds complexity.
- **Light**: Baseline +1 tier. Wavelength precision required.

**Structural Disciplines (Elevated Baseline)**
- **Binding**: Baseline +1.5 tiers. Relational interactions cost more than physical.

**Consciousness Disciplines (High Baseline)**
- **Mind**: Baseline +2 tiers. Consciousness targeting requires extreme precision.
- **Soul**: Baseline +2.5 tiers. The most expensive discipline baseline.

## Shape Multipliers

**Simple Shapes**
- **Triangle** (3 outer points): ×1.0 multiplier
- **Square** (4 outer points): ×1.2 multiplier

**Complex Shapes**
- **Pentagon** (5 outer points): ×1.5 multiplier
- **Hexagon** (6 outer points): ×1.8 multiplier
- **Circle** (6 outer + center): ×2.0 multiplier

## Variable Specification Costs

**[[Reach]] Specifications**
- Self: +0 tiers
- Touch: +0.5 tiers
- Short: +0.5 tiers
- Medium: +1 tier
- Long: +1.5 tiers
- Line-of-Sight: +2 tiers
- Linked: +2.5 tiers

**[[Persistence]] Specifications**
- Immediate: +0 tiers
- Timed Short: +0.5 tiers
- Timed Medium: +1 tier
- Timed Long: +1.5 tiers
- Sustained: +1 tier (ongoing)
- Permanent: +3 tiers (during cast only)

**[[Geometry Pattern]] Specifications**
- Point: +0 tiers
- Beam: +0.5 tiers
- Cone: +1 tier
- Ring: +1 tier
- Sphere: +1.5 tiers
- Cylinder: +1.5 tiers
- Field: +2 tiers

**[[Target Spec]] Specifications**
- Written: +0 tiers
- Self: +0 tiers
- Individual: +0.5 tiers
- Object: +0.5 tiers
- Group: +1 tier
- Filter: +2 tiers

**[[Output Mode]] Cross-Discipline Penalties**
- Natural pairing (Heat→Thermal, Force→Kinetic): +0 tiers
- Compatible pairing (Electric→Kinetic, Chemical→Constraint): +0.5 tiers
- Incompatible pairing (Mind→Kinetic, Soul→Constraint): +1.5 tiers

- Standard warming sigil

- Base: Soul (2.5) × Circle (2.0) + Line-of-Sight (2) + Filter (2) = T9
- Advanced soul manipulation

## Efficiency Factors

**Experience Reduction**
Practitioners with extensive experience in specific spell combinations can reduce effective costs by 0.5-1 tier through refined channeling technique.

**Prepared Sigil Reduction**
Pre-inscribed [[Sigils]] reduce activation cost by approximately 1 tier compared to improvised casting, as the geometric framework absorbs part of the precision demand.

**Environmental Factors**
- High ambient [[Flux]] areas: -0.5 tier reduction
- Flux-dead zones: +1 tier penalty
- Dimensional boundary proximity: +0.5 tier penalty
- Prepared casting circles: -0.5 tier reduction

## Practical Applications

**[[Flux Users|Formulist]] Work Planning**
A T3 practitioner can safely execute spells with total costs up to T3. Working at T2.5 or below allows sustained casting without exhaustion.

**[[Control Tier|Tier]] Assessment Implications**
Children assessed at specific tiers can expect to handle spell combinations within these cost ranges:
- T1: Basic utility spells only
- T2: Standard working magic
- T3: Intermediate applications
- T4+: Advanced and specialized casting

**Combat Considerations**
High-stress casting typically adds +0.5 tier penalty due to reduced precision under pressure. Combat-viable spells should be at least 1 tier below practitioner maximum.
