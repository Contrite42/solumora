[[Guilds]] is a broad reference for organized trade and professional bodies across [[Solumora]].

In most Terravellian contexts, "guilds" points to the specific systems documented under [[Trade Guilds]]. In Auralis, equivalent functions may be split between guild-like associations and council-approved registries.

When pages use the generic term, treat this note as a bridge to the detailed institutional page.
