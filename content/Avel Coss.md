Avel Coss has guided eleven crossings of [[Equatorial Desert|Desert Zakros]]. He is forty-four, [[Control Tier|T4]], and works from the [[Auralis]] side, out of a border settlement he uses as a business address and rarely as a home. He has rules about which crossings he will take. They are not negotiable and he does not explain them in detail to prospective clients, though the clients who ask specific questions get more specific answers than those who ask general ones.

The rules come from eleven completed crossings and two that he counts differently — one that turned back by his decision, one by circumstance. He has brought forty-seven people across in the eleven completed trips. He brought forty-seven back. This record is his credential and he does not treat it carelessly.

His casting is used for the work the desert specifically requires: environmental reading at range, heat management, emergency medical applications that he learned outside any formal curriculum. He certified in none of these categories as they are formally defined. He has a T4 general certification and seventeen years of experience and has found that combination more useful than any specialist credential.

He does not consider himself part of [[Auralis]]'s expedition network, though the network refers clients to him and has twice attempted to formalize the arrangement into something with reporting obligations. He declined both times without extensive explanation. The referrals continue regardless.

He has written about what he knows of the desert — not for any particular reader, as a way of thinking through what eleven crossings have taught him and what they have not. The desert in the crossing season is specific enough that writing is the only way he has found to hold all of it clearly at once.
