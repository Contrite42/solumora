**Tolla Rend** is forty-six years old and works as a dock supervisor at [[Hedun]]'s harbor. [[Control Tier|T1]]. She has been in harbor work for twenty-two years, moving from line worker to shift lead to her current role over a decade and a half of demonstrated competence and an instinct for managing the logistics problems that harbor work generates without warning.

Her view of Hedun is the one the bluff district establishments do not cultivate: the city runs on the harbor and the harbor runs on people who work long days handling cargo that the bluff's clientele never touches. She is not resentful about this. The division of labor is clear and she occupies her side of it with complete understanding of what her side is. She has been to the bluff district perhaps a dozen times in her adult life, at celebrations, and found it pleasant in the way of places designed to be pleasant. She prefers the harbor in the early morning, when the light is right and the crew has the efficiency of people who have done the same work together for years.

She has known [[Toven Ral]] by sight and occasional conversation for years — he lives in the city, the harbor is one of the places the city's residents pass through. She finds him unremarkable in the specific way of a city that has processed enough unusual people to stop finding them unusual. Whatever he was before he was a Hedun resident is not something she has thought about.

Her T1 casting is used for cargo seal verification — the minimum Flux application required for certain documentation in Hedun's commercial framework. She uses it accurately and without interest. It is a credential the work requires.
