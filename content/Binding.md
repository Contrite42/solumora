[[Binding]] is the discipline of constraint in the [[Sigils]] system: making things hold, stay, seal, lock, or remain connected under stress. It is expensive because the spell must maintain a stable rule over time rather than create a brief effect.

In practical use, Binding appears less as spectacle and more as infrastructure. Crossings, cargo control, temporary fortification, and emergency containment all rely on reliable constraint work. Many "safe" procedures in dangerous environments are actually Binding protocols maintained by teams rather than single casters.

In [[Spell Variables]], Binding is a high-cost discipline multiplier. Its effects become especially demanding when combined with long [[Persistence]] states (such as latched or permanent) or wide [[Pattern]] geometries.

Typical Binding uses include:
- Sealing seams, vents, and joints against heat or particulate intrusion.
- Locking temporary route markers and hazard boundaries into place.
- Reinforcing structures under motion, load, or weather stress.
