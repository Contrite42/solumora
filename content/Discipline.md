[[Discipline]] defines the fundamental *method* a spell uses to interact with reality. It is the “filter” that determines what rules the spell obeys and what kind of manipulation it performs. If [[Output Mode]] is the payload (what manifests), Discipline is the interface (how Flux grabs the world).

When Discipline is not specified by the spell’s [[Shape]], it defaults to Raw.

## Disciplines (Scholar Standard)

- Raw  
  Unfiltered [[Flux]]. It produces blunt, generic effects and is easier to copy, but is less efficient and less precise than specialized disciplines.
![[Discipline__RAW 1.png]]
- Force  
  Manipulates momentum, pressure, inertia, and motion. Used for pushes, lifts, impacts, barriers, and controlled movement when paired with Move.
![[Discipline__FORCE 1.png]]
- Heat  
  Manipulates heat transfer and temperature gradients. Used for warming, cooling, ignition, thermal sealing, and phase-change effects.
![[Discipline__HEAT 2.png]]
- Electric  
  Manipulates charge, current, and electromagnetic flow. Used for shocks, arcs, disruption, and powering/interrupting mechanisms.
![[Discipline__ELEC 1.png]]
- Chemical  
  Manipulates reaction conditions and material chemistry. Used for corrosion, neutralization, catalysis, smoke/fumes, adhesives, and controlled decomposition.
![[Discipline__CHEM 1.png]]
- Light  
  Manipulates photons and visibility. Used for illumination, glare, concealment, projection, mirage effects, and “line-of-sight” clarity spells.
![[Discipline__LIGHT 1.png]]
- Sound  
  Manipulates vibration and pressure waves. Used for silence, resonance, communication, disorientation, and structural harmonic tricks.
![[Discipline__SOUND 1.png]]
- Mind  
  Manipulates attention, perception, interpretation, and nervous-system signaling. Used for distraction, suggestion, sensory filtering, pain/fear triggers, and detection keyed to intent.
![[Discipline__MIND 1.png]]
- Binding  
  Manipulates constraints, permissions, attachment, and rule-locking. Used for wards, seals, tethers, locks, suppression fields, and “only/except” behavior when paired with Filter.
![[Discipline__BIND 1.png]]
- Soul  
  Manipulates identity, continuity, imprint, and the “true signature” of beings and objects. Used for oaths, unbreakable marks, severance, restoration of identity, and effects that persist beyond physical change.
![[Discipline__SOUL 1.png]]
## Discipline vs [[Output Mode]]

- [[Discipline]] = *how* the spell touches reality (rules + interface).  
- [[Output Mode]] = *what* it emits or applies (payload).

Example: Mind + Thermal can mean heat output that is keyed to attention/fear/pain signals rather than a simple “heat the air” effect.

## Practical notes

- Most mass-produced [[Grimoires]] favor disciplines with predictable failure modes (Force, Heat, Binding]]) and avoid disciplines with complex ethics or ambiguity (Mind, Soul) unless restricted.  
- Higher [[Control Tier]] does not automatically make a Discipline stronger—it makes it easier to use safely and precisely.
