**Discipline** is the fundamental variable in any [[Flux]] casting — the aspect of reality the spell will interact with. Every [[Sigils|sigil]] must specify exactly one discipline, which occupies one of the shape's outer variable slots and determines the base cost multiplier for the entire effect.

The discipline system recognizes nine distinct categories of [[Flux]] interaction, each requiring different levels of precision from the ambient field. A spell that moves objects ([[Force]]) asks less of the Flux than a spell that reads thoughts ([[Mind]]) or manipulates soul-signature ([[Soul]]). This difference in precision demand is reflected in the cost multiplier each discipline applies to the [[Shape]] base cost.

## Disciplines

**Raw (×1)** — Unfiltered [[Flux]] manipulation. No specific targeting or discrimination required. Used for effects that channel pure energy without asking the Flux to distinguish between different types of matter or different aspects of reality. The baseline discipline.

**Force (×2)** — Kinetic energy and mechanical interaction. Moving objects, applying pressure, creating barriers, generating impact. The Flux must distinguish between matter and empty space but does not need to discriminate between different types of matter.

**Heat (×2)** — Thermal energy manipulation. Heating, cooling, temperature control, thermal detection. The Flux must interact with molecular motion specifically, but thermal effects are relatively straightforward for the field to produce.

**Light (×3)** — Photonic energy and electromagnetic radiation in the visible spectrum. Illumination, focused beams, optical effects. Requires more precision than thermal work because light behavior is more complex than heat transfer.

**Sound (×4)** — Acoustic energy and vibration control. Sound generation, noise suppression, acoustic detection, vibration manipulation. The Flux must interact with compression waves through various media, requiring careful calibration.

**Electric (×5)** — Electrical energy and electromagnetic effects beyond the visible spectrum. Shock, electromagnetic pulse, electrical detection, current control. High precision required due to the specific nature of electrical phenomena.

**Chemical (×5)** — Molecular structure and chemical reaction control. Catalysis, reaction suppression, molecular detection, chemical transformation. The Flux must distinguish between different molecular structures and their interaction patterns.

**Binding (×10)** — Constraint and connection effects. Seals, locks, adhesion, structural reinforcement, connection between objects or effects. Requires the Flux to create persistent relationships between separate entities.

**Mind (×25)** — Neural activity and mental state interaction. Thought reading, memory access, emotional influence, mental detection, consciousness effects. The Flux must distinguish between different types of neural activity and interact with brain states specifically.

**Soul (×75)** — Soul-signature and identity manipulation. Identity reading, soul marking, essence detection, fundamental identity alteration. The finest discrimination the Flux system supports — the field must locate and interact with the specific signature that defines individual identity.

## Cost Multipliers

Each discipline multiplies the [[Shape]] base [[Watts|W]] cost to produce the core Flux demand for any spell. A Triangle Raw spell starts at 3×1 = 3W. A Triangle Soul spell starts at 3×75 = 225W before any other variables are added.

The multiplier gap reflects the precision the Flux field must maintain to execute the effect. Raw discipline requires no discriminating precision — unfiltered Flux channeled without asking the field to distinguish between different aspects of reality. Soul discipline requires the finest discrimination the Flux system supports — the field must find and interact with soul-signature specifically, distinguishing individual identity from all other aspects of a person's existence.

This precision demand means that higher-multiplier disciplines floor their total cost above tiers that common practitioners can access. A Triangle Soul spell costs 225W minimum — already T3 — before any variable additions. A [[Circle]] Soul spell costs 4,125W minimum — T6 floor — before variables. Mass [[Grimoires]] favor Force, Heat, and Binding (×2, ×2, ×10) because their multipliers produce effects at tiers common practitioners can reach. Mind (×25) and Soul (×75) floor their total cost above T3–T4 even in the simplest Triangle configurations.

## Discipline vs Output Mode

Discipline determines what aspect of reality the spell interacts with. [[Output Mode]] determines what form that interaction takes when it manifests. A Force discipline spell naturally produces kinetic effects — movement, pressure, impact. But Force discipline can be combined with thermal [[Output Mode]] to produce kinetic energy that manifests as heat, or with photonic Output Mode to produce kinetic energy that manifests as light.

Cross-discipline output modes carry additional cost beyond the base discipline multiplier. Natural pairings (Force→kinetic, Heat→thermal, Light→photonic) carry no additional cost. Adjacent disciplines (Heat→kinetic, Force→thermal) add +10W. Cross-type combinations (Mind→kinetic, Force→neural) add +30W. Extreme crosses (Soul→kinetic, Force→soul) add +60W.

The discipline multiplier applies to the Shape base cost. Output Mode costs are flat additions applied after the core calculation. A Triangle Force spell with thermal output costs (3×2) + 10 = 16W total.
