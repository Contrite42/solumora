**Veld Dorv** is a field documentation officer attached to [[The Advancement Corps]]' Emberfall-facing operations. [[Control Tier|T2]]. His work is routine on paper: intake, chain-of-custody checks, report reconciliation, and archive transfer verification.

Veld's practical value is pattern memory. He notices when expected reports do not arrive, when filing language shifts between parallel operations, and when archive destinations change without matching administrative notices. He has not publicized these observations, but he has started keeping a private discrepancy ledger keyed to expedition windows and route codes.

He is not trying to expose anyone. He is trying to keep records coherent enough that he can still trust his own job outputs. In a system where selective omission is normal, that alone makes him important.
