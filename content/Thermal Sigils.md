[[Thermal Sigils]] are practical spell patterns used for heat buffering, insulation control, and temperature stabilization across people, cargo, and structures.

They are central to desert crossing, cold-route operations, and warehouse protection workflows where thermal drift causes cascading losses.

Most thermal kits prioritize reliability over peak output, since sustained moderation is more useful than brief extremes.
