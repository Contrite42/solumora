# GM Session Hooks

Use this page as the canonical pattern for gameable blocks on lore pages.

## Standard Block Pattern

### Use In Session

- Immediate scene entry point.
- What players can do in 10 minutes.
- What changes if they do nothing.

### Complications

- One institutional obstacle.
- One interpersonal obstacle.
- One logistics/time obstacle.

### Secrets

- One truth known by power holders.
- One truth known by workers/operators.
- One truth discoverable only through contradiction.

## Regional Encounter Seed Pattern

- Trigger: what makes this situation happen now.
- Stakes: who gains, who loses, who is trapped.
- Twist: what appears true but is incomplete.
- Exit conditions: how the scene resolves into next decisions.

## Regional Encounter Seeds (Phase 1)

### Ashford: Paper-Clean, Behavior-Dirty

- Trigger: A routine cargo clears on paper but triggers unusual secondary questions.
- Stakes: Merchant contract penalties vs. checkpoint trust.
- Twist: The suspicious element is not forged paperwork, but synchronized behavior.
- Exit conditions: Resolve as false alarm, targeted probe, or evidence of wider procedural drift.

### Solhaven: Ceremony Window Failure

- Trigger: A critical registration/inscription case arrives at the edge of allowable timing.
- Stakes: Legal identity continuity and lifetime ledger integrity.
- Twist: The formal delay source is compliant, but practically incompatible with real conditions.
- Exit conditions: Secure compliant exception, accept irreversible administrative damage, or expose process conflict.

### Valdenmoor: Guild-Safe, Worker-Unsafe

- Trigger: A certified operation passes audit while frontline workers report repeat near-failures.
- Stakes: Production continuity vs. preventable injury risk.
- Twist: Every single record is technically valid; only cross-record patterning reveals the problem.
- Exit conditions: Patch locally, escalate institutionally, or document for later leverage.
