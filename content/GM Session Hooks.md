# GM Session Hooks

Use this page as the canonical pattern for gameable blocks on lore pages.

## Standard Block Pattern

### Use In Session

- Immediate scene entry point.
- What players can do in 10 minutes.
- What changes if they do nothing.

### Complications

- One institutional obstacle.
- One interpersonal obstacle.
- One logistics/time obstacle.

### Secrets

- One truth known by power holders.
- One truth known by workers/operators.
- One truth discoverable only through contradiction.

## Regional Encounter Seed Pattern

- Trigger: what makes this situation happen now.
- Stakes: who gains, who loses, who is trapped.
- Twist: what appears true but is incomplete.
- Exit conditions: how the scene resolves into next decisions.
