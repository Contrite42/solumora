**Tovan Ashce** _(Council Member, Auralis — Cassia's Faction)_

Tovan Ashce is twenty-eight years old. He is the fourth generation of his family to hold a seat on [[The Council of Auralis]], and he carries that institutional weight the way some people carry age they haven't earned yet — naturally, without apparent effort, in a way that makes the people around him faintly uncomfortable.

Talking to Tovan is like talking to someone who has already had this conversation several times before and is being patient about having it again.

## Character

Tovan is not warm. He is not cold either — he is precise, which is a different thing. He follows a conversation faster than people expect, reaches conclusions before they are stated, and has the particular stillness of someone who stopped being surprised by most things quite early. He does not show off. He simply operates at a level that occasionally makes itself visible whether he intends it to or not.

His [[Flux]] ability is negligible by Auralis standards — he has never tested above T2, which in a culture that ties social worth to casting ability should be a significant disadvantage. It is not, because Tovan is the kind of person for whom it becomes immediately clear that his ability is not the most relevant thing about him.

## The Ashce Position

The Ashce family has been part of Auralis governance for four generations because they believe, consistently and without drama, that good governance is the precondition for everything else the kingdom wants to do. Not ideology, not ambition, not magical advancement — just competent, stable, honest rule.

Tovan holds this position the way you hold something you were given young and examined carefully and kept because it held up. He is not a traditionalist in the sense of wanting things to stay the same. He is a traditionalist in the sense of believing that certain principles existed before [[Drest]]'s council and should exist after it.

His opposition to the current council direction is not theatrical. He does not argue loudly or position himself as a rebel. He votes strategically, withholds information selectively, and has twice blocked actions that would have reduced [[Cassia]]'s formal authority — both times through procedural means that left no clean target for retaliation.

## The Observation He Has Not Made Out Loud

Tovan has noticed that [[Cassia]] and [[Drest]] are remarkably similar. Not in manner — they are quite different in manner — but in the quality of their attention, the way they process a room, a certain characteristic patience that looks like stillness and isn't. He has filed this observation where he files things that are interesting but not yet useful. He has not mentioned it to anyone.

He does not know whether this observation means what he thinks it means. He suspects it does.

## Relationship with Cassia

Tovan's support for [[Cassia]] is the quietest of her three allies and the most structurally useful. He does not advocate for her publicly — that would draw attention in ways that cost more than they gain. He simply makes things difficult for the people who want to reduce her authority, through means that are always technically defensible.

Cassia knows what he is doing. She has never thanked him directly, which he considers correct. Gratitude acknowledged is gratitude that can be used against both of them.
