A service broker in [[Solhaven]]'s fourth terrace filed a private dispute after noticing two long-term clients gave conflicting recollections of a woman they both claimed to have met monthly for years. The conflict was precise rather than dramatic: one remembered a left-handed signer, one remembered right-handed; one remembered a widow's family story, one remembered no family details at all.

The broker's first assumption was fabricated credentials. He pulled three years of appointment cards and found perfect payment continuity. There was no missed schedule, no forged seal, and no obvious financial motive.

He drafted a quiet referral to a guild verification contact, then withdrew it the next day after an unplanned social dinner where the same woman appeared and resolved a shipping dispute with unusual tact. By the end of the evening, both clients agreed they had probably misremembered small details under stress.

The broker burned the draft referral to avoid insulting a reliable account network. The woman continued attending selected terrace meetings for another season before disappearing from that circuit.

No one in the dispute record used the name [[Mave]].
