Lenne Sosk works cargo handling for a mid-sized importing house in [[Solhaven]]'s harbor district, and has since she was nineteen. She is thirty-one. The importing house handles preserved goods from [[Terravelle]] — food stocks, certain materials that keep better in Terravellian cold-storage sigil arrangements than in anything Auralis has developed for the southern climate. The work is physical, the hours are consistent, and the foreman she reports to leaves her alone when the work is done correctly, which it always is.

She assessed [[Control Tier|T0]] at ten years old in the Luminant ceremony her family attended. She remembers the ceremony clearly — the way the room smelled, the particular quality of light through the windows, the assessor's hands — and the walk home afterward, her mother holding her hand and not saying anything for a long time, and then saying something ordinary about dinner, which Lenne has always considered the right decision.

She has been offered a supervisory position twice. The position comes with more visibility and less predictability. She has declined both times.

She participates in the [[Ascendant Path]]'s ceremonies when they occur among people she knows — assessments, marriages, occasionally a death. She participates correctly. She has found that participating correctly costs less than the alternative and produces approximately the same outcome as everything else she has tried.

She has three people she is close to, all in her building, all of whom understand the harbor district in roughly the way she does. They eat together on rest days. On rest days the harbor district is quieter and the light is different and it is possible to believe, for a few hours, that the week ahead will be slightly less than the week behind.
