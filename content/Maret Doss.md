**Maret Doss** is forty-four years old and farms a small holding in the eastern agricultural belt of [[Auralis]], two days' travel inland from the coast. The holding was her parents' before it was hers. She expects it will be someone else's after.

Her [[Control Tier|T1]] casting ability is used almost entirely for irrigation work — augmenting flow to the drier sections of the property during the dry season, a practical application that requires minimal technique and delivers reliable return. She received no formal training. Her mother showed her the method, which her mother had learned from a neighbor decades earlier. She has passed the same method to the one hired hand who has stayed long enough to learn it.

The [[Ascendant Path]] runs in the background of her life the way weather does — present, occasionally relevant, shaping certain rhythms without being the thing she thinks about. The local Luminant holds ceremonies at the appropriate intervals. She attends the ones for planting and harvest because these are the ones that mean something concrete about the year's work. The others she attends when the timing is convenient.

She had two children. The younger tested [[Control Tier|T3]] at assessment and left for [[Solhaven]] three years ago. She understood why he left. She misses him and these are not the same feeling, and she holds them as not the same feeling.

The older child tested T0 and stayed and manages the holding's records with a precision that Maret, who is not naturally organized, finds quietly impressive. She does not say this aloud because the older child would find it embarrassing.
