[[Food Creation]] refers to Flux-assisted methods that increase short-term edible output when conventional provisioning is constrained.

In practice, most legal methods are augmentation and preservation workflows, not creation from nothing. High-efficiency emergency outputs are possible but expensive and usually inferior to standard prepared stocks.

Because sustained food logistics are cheaper through agriculture and trade, food-creation spellwork is treated as contingency infrastructure.
