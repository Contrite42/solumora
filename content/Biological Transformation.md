[[Biological Transformation]] is a high-risk application class where [[Flux]] is used to alter living tissue states beyond normal healing adjustments.

In mainstream practice across [[Terravelle]] and [[Auralis]], this work is tightly limited by ethics, cost, and failure risk. Most institutions treat full-transform operations as experimental research rather than routine craft.

Field references to biological transformation usually indicate theory work, failed prototypes, or restricted archives rather than available public techniques.
