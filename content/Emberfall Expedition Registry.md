The Emberfall Expedition Registry is the permit and compliance office for sanctioned desert work routed through [[Emberfall]].

If a crossing team wants sponsor coverage, it starts here.

## Funding

Funding comes from permit fees, sponsor bonds, and council project allocations tied to research agendas backed by [[The Culmination Faction]].

## Recruitment

The Registry hires route auditors, field scribes, and incident coders with prior crossing exposure and strong documentation discipline.

## Rules

- no expedition leaves without registered route window
- incident logs are mandatory for rift contact or route loss
- high-risk teams must carry verified survival grimoires

## Opposition

Independent operators who profit from unlogged crossings bypass the Registry when possible, especially when schedule pressure is high.

## What It Trades In

It trades in permission, insurance eligibility, and institutional memory about what actually fails in the [[Equatorial Desert]].
