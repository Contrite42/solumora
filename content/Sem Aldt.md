**Sem Aldt** is twenty-nine years old and crews a coastal trading vessel that runs the northern [[Terravelle]] coastline between the river ports south of [[Valdenmoor]] and the harbor settlements north of [[Greyveil]]. [[Control Tier|T1]]. He has been working this route for six years. Before that, two years on a longer ocean run out of a southern Terravellian port, which he left because the pay was marginally better on the coastal route and the time away from port was shorter, and at twenty-three those things mattered in the order he weighted them.

Maritime work on the northern coast is seasonal in ways the river trade is not. The summer months have reliable traffic and reasonable weather. The shoulder seasons are the skilled-sailor months — the runs that require good judgment about when to go and when to stay — and the deep winter is the maintenance season, when the vessels are in port and the crew does repair work and the work of building relationships with the harbor operators that smooth the summer season's logistics.

His T1 casting is used for weather reading — a standard maritime application, giving sailors a short-range forecast of the actual conditions rather than the general seasonal pattern. T1 weather reading is limited in range and certainty. Sem treats it as one input among several: his own experience of how this stretch of coast behaves, the harbor operators' local knowledge, the other sailors' observations. He does not rely on it more than it warrants.

He is aware that the northern runs have become more complicated in the past year — more documentation required at the ports near [[Greyveil]], more inspection of cargo manifests, more questions about origin and destination that the cargo has always had clear answers to. He does not know why this has changed. He notes it as a change and adjusts accordingly.
