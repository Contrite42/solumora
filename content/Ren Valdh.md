**Ren Valdh** is twenty-six years old and works in [[The Council of Auralis|Council]] administrative services in [[Solhaven]]'s Crestward district. [[Control Tier|T5]]. She was raised in a Crestward family — her mother holds a minor administrative appointment, her father's position is in the [[Auralis]] commercial registry — and assessed T5 at eleven with the institutional attention that T5 produces in Solhaven: the Path registration, the scholarship offers, the particular way that adults around her began speaking about her in the third person while she was still in the room.

She understood, before she understood much else about the city, that her tier was an asset belonging to the family and the Path and the institutions before it belonged to her. She has spent fifteen years negotiating this understanding. The negotiation has not resolved. She still works in the Council offices and attends the Path ceremonies and is present at the Crestward gatherings where the city's actual decisions are made in passing. She is good at all of this and cannot determine whether being good at it is something she chose.

Her administrative work is technically sound and politically competent. She understands the Council's formal structure and its informal one — who holds authority on paper and who holds it in practice, which decisions happen in the official sessions and which happen in the conversations before them. She has been acquiring this understanding deliberately for five years. She does not know yet what she intends to do with it.

The question she has not answered is whether she is building something or becoming something she did not decide to become. The city's architecture does not make this question easy to hold. In Solhaven, the difference between agency and accommodation looks, from outside, identical.
