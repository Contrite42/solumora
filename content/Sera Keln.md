_(Council Member, Auralis — Culmination Faction)_

**Sera Keln** is a Senior Scholar from [[Emberfall]] and the council's most credible academic voice on the ruins research. She joined [[The Council of Auralis]] eight years ago at [[Drest|Drest's]] invitation and has not changed her methodology since, which is the thing about her that makes the Culmination Faction both valuable to have and difficult to fully manage. She believes in the desert goal because the evidence she has spent her career examining points toward something real. She does not believe in it because Drest does.

## Character

Sera Keln is fifty-four, thorough, and entirely unimpressed by conviction that cannot show its work. She does not dismiss faith as a motivation — she has watched true believers fund expeditions that recovered genuine finds, and she is not naive about how research gets resourced — but she applies the same evaluative framework to everything, including the Culmination Faction's conclusions. This has produced, over eight years, a relationship with [[Drest]] that is mutually useful and periodically tense.

She is compact in manner: short sentences, specific references, no interest in persuading people who are not listening carefully. She gives a full account of her evidence exactly once. People who ask follow-up questions get complete answers. People who did not listen the first time do not get another chance at it.

Her [[Flux]] ability sits at T5 — she came up through Scholarly practice rather than combat training, and her casting is research-oriented. She uses detection and analysis sigils at the Rare tier and considers this sufficient.

## The Academic Work

Sera Keln has spent twenty years on the inscription fragments recovered from [[Equatorial Desert|Desert Zakros]] expedition sites. Her progress on partial translation exceeds that of any other active Scholar in [[Auralis]], which means she is closer than anyone else in a formal academic context to the truth of what the ruins represent — and still substantially short of it.

What she has: fragmentary phrases that suggest a Flux system of greater complexity than the current one, sigil geometry from confirmed sites that includes configurations outside the current [[Shape|Shape framework]], structural analysis of the ruins' physical geometry that implies multi-level construction techniques beyond current capability. What she does not have: a working translation of more than scattered phrases, a complete sigil pattern from any single site, or any explanation for the absence of bodies.

She is more careful about her conclusions than the Culmination Faction prefers. She has published three papers at the Emberhall that qualified findings the faction had been citing as confirmed. [[Drest]] has not mentioned these papers to her directly. She is aware he has not mentioned them.

## On the Goal

Sera Keln does not dismiss the Culmination goal. The evidence for a sophisticated, significantly advanced pre-modern civilization is real. Whether that civilization achieved what the Culmination Faction believes it was working toward is a question she considers open and possibly unanswerable with current evidence.

What she will not do is state a conclusion she does not have evidence for, including in council sessions. This is what makes her more dangerous to the faction than its true believers: a believer will rationalize findings that don't fit. Sera Keln publishes them.
