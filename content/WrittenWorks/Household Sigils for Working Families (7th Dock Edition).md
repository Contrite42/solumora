## Foreword to the Seventh Dock Edition

This book is for households that need working, low-cost, reliable daily [[Sigils|sigil]] use. Every page in this edition was selected for materials commonly available in district markets. Every spell here can be maintained by low-tier [[Flux Users|casters]] without advanced tutoring.

If your copy has missing strokes or blurred corners, return it. Bad copying costs more than good books.

---

## Plate 1: [[Common Grimoire#Warmstone|Warmstone]]

Purpose: Holds steady warmth in cookware, bedbricks, and night-room basins.

House Rule:

- Activate only after checking container integrity.
- Do not leave active around dry cloth stacks.

Margin note (previous owner):
"Night use only in our house. Day use wasted fuel and Watts."

## Plate 2: [[Common Grimoire#Breathclean|Breathclean]]

Purpose: Filters smoke and fine dust for enclosed rooms.

Use sequence:

1. Open vent.
2. Activate at low intensity.
3. Seal room only after smoke drops.

Margin note:
"Works well with fish-smoke days near docks. Not enough for chemical spill nights."

## Plate 3: [[Common Grimoire#Mending Seal|Mending Seal]]

Purpose: Temporary repair for cracked pottery, split handles, and light vessel seams.

Restriction:

- Not valid for pressure vessels.
- Replace with physical repair within seven days.

Margin note:
"Held our basin three weeks. We got lucky."

## Plate 4: [[Common Grimoire#Glowmark|Glowmark]]

Purpose: Low-cost light marking for stairs, doorframes, and ledger shelves.

Pattern recommendation:

- Keep marks at knee height for children and elders.
- Use paired marks at entries/exits.

Margin note:
"Blue mark for back stairs, amber for roof stairs."

## Plate 5: [[Common Grimoire#Lockward|Lockward]]

Purpose: Basic anti-tamper ward for trunks and cabinet doors.

Warning:

- Not a defense against trained thieves.
- Designed to reveal entry, not prevent professional bypass.

Margin note:
"Good enough for shared-house kitchen chest."
