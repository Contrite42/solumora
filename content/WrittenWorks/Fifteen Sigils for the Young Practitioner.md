## A Note from the Committee

The fifteen sigils in this collection were selected for a specific purpose: to teach the fundamentals of [[Flux]] work in a sequence that builds skill rather than demonstrating capability. They are not the most impressive T1 spells available. They are the ones that teach something, in an order that makes the next one easier.

We have included teaching notes after each sigil. They reflect common errors observed across multiple apprentice cohorts. If your student makes an error that is not in our notes, document it and send it to the committee. The next edition benefits from your experience.

*Teaching note: All defaults. The student writes the sigil, provides Flux, and light appears. There is nothing to configure. ## On What Comes Next

The fifteen sigils here represent T1 competency. A student who has worked through them in sequence, understood the teaching notes, and can cast all fifteen cleanly is ready for the [[Trade Guilds|Journeyman]] assessment track.

What the Journeyman track will require: sustained persistence over longer periods, Square inscriptions with additional variable slots, Chemical discipline at working depth, and the beginning of understanding how spells interact with each other. The foundation built in this selection is designed to support that next step.

Work through this slowly. The temptation to move faster than understanding allows is the most common error the committee has observed across cohorts. Speed comes from depth, not from skipping.
