**Lenna Cors** is fourteen years old and was assessed [[Control Tier|T4]] three months ago at a Path chapter in a mid-sized [[Auralis]] city. Her mother assessed T2. Her father assessed T1. No one in her immediate family had a T4 result in the generation before, or the one before that.

The institutional attention that followed her assessment has not stopped. The second assessor confirmed within two weeks. The scholarship correspondence arrived within a month — three letters, from institutions in [[Solhaven]], [[Emberfall]], and one she had not previously heard of. A senior Luminant from the city's main Path chapter visited her family's home for a pastoral call, which in practice meant a two-hour conversation that her parents attended and that was ostensibly about spiritual preparation for the advancement track and was in practice about something more specific. She is not certain what.

She was, before the assessment, a person who went to school and helped with her family's supply business and had a group of five friends she has known since she was eight. The assessment has not changed any of this structurally. She still goes to school. She still helps with the business. Four of the five friends treat her exactly as before. The fifth has become slightly formal in ways that she cannot figure out how to address directly.

The letters sit in a box in her room. She has read each of them twice. She is not ready to respond to any of them. She is also aware — from the Luminant's visit, from her parents' careful conversations, from everything the city's implicit communication about tier is designed to convey — that not responding is itself a decision, and that the time available for it is finite.

She would like someone to explain to her what she has actually become without also explaining what she is supposed to do about it. This is not, as far as she can tell, a thing anyone will do.
