Sable Morn runs the [[Valdenmoor]]–[[Ashford]]–[[Greyveil]] circuit, three to four times a year depending on cargo and road conditions. She is thirty-seven, [[Control Tier|T2]], and has been doing this work since she was twenty-two. She trades in preserved goods, equipment fittings, and the occasional consignment of documents that clients prefer not to move through official channels. She does not ask what the documents contain.

She drives her own wagon and manages her own accounts. She has working relationships with three stable operators across the circuit, two dock masters in Ashford, and a family of outfitters in Greyveil who consistently have what she needs for the northern leg. She does not employ anyone. The circuit is sized for one person to run competently, and she has found competence easier to maintain without the additional variable of someone else's judgment applied to her decisions.

She has kept a route journal since her third year on the circuit. It began as a practical document — road conditions, timing, costs, notes on which wayhouses were reliable — and has accumulated into something more specific than a ledger. Fifteen years of the same roads in different seasons, the same towns in different situations. She notices things. The journal notices them with her.

Her [[Control Tier|T2]] casting is used for cargo work — minor stabilizing applications, weight distribution adjustments that keep the load from shifting on difficult stretches. She certified for this specifically, which took two years longer than she expected and cost more than she had planned for. She uses it every day and does not think about it much.

She owns a small property at the Valdenmoor end of the circuit that she uses between runs. She has not spent more than six consecutive weeks there in the past decade.
