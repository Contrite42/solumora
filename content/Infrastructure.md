[[Infrastructure]] in Solumora refers to the systems that keep movement, settlement function, and service continuity stable under stress.

This includes roads, ports, distribution depots, registry processes, and the low-tier maintenance spellwork that prevents cascading failure.

Infrastructure is usually politically invisible until it breaks.
