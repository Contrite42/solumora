**Orva Dresk** is an [[Auralis]] researcher based in [[Solhaven]]'s [[Crestward District]], working on [[Hypertext]] reconstruction from secondhand sources. She is thirty-four, T4, and has been working on this project for six years without Scholarium access.

## The Project

Orva has never been to [[Wolfpoint]]. Her application for Scholarium access was declined eight years ago — the Voss family's evaluation process found her research proposal insufficiently developed, which was probably accurate. She has been working ever since from what she can obtain through alternative means.

Her sources: the published writings of practitioners who have passed through the Scholarium (sparse, restricted by access agreements, and carefully stripped of operational specifics before publication); interviews with [[Crel Aldst]] and similar practitioners who returned from Wolfpoint with partial knowledge and are willing to discuss what they learned in terms that do not technically violate their access agreements; analysis of the Hypertext symbol sequences visible in ruins artifacts that Auralis's expeditions have documented, which she has compared systematically against the standard [[Spell Variables]] notation framework.

The ruins-artifact analysis is where she has made genuine progress. Hypertext sequences inscribed in functioning ruins artifacts — mechanisms that are still operating, in some cases, despite centuries of decay — provide direct evidence of how the symbol system works when it is working. She has reverse-engineered approximately forty percent of what she believes to be the foundational structure. The remaining sixty percent requires either Scholarium access or a source of ruins artifacts substantially better than what [[The Advancement Corps]] has published.

## Position and Tension

Orva holds a nominal position in the Crestward Research Nexus on a project that is officially labeled as "ancient civilization communication systems analysis." The Culmination Faction has funded this project at modest levels — enough to maintain her position, not enough to provide the resources her reconstruction work actually requires. She suspects this is deliberate: the Culmination Faction wants the project to continue at a pace they can monitor without it producing results quickly enough to reduce their leverage over the Hypertext question.

She is aware that [[Mira Solv]] has direct access to Wolfpoint and has declined to share Hypertext theory with Auralis's research institutions. She is aware that if she could make contact with Mira Solv through channels other than the formal Scholarium route, she might be able to obtain the information her reconstruction project is missing. She has not pursued this, partly because the risks of unauthorized Scholarium contact are significant and partly because she is not confident Mira Solv would respond as she hopes.

[[Pellam Vores]], a colleague in the Research Nexus, has recently been assigned to a project investigating Flux incursion mechanisms near anomalous ruins sites. Orva considers this potentially relevant to her own work and has been trying to arrange an information exchange that the Nexus's internal protocols make awkward.

## Character

Orva is driven and precise in ways that make her difficult to work alongside on projects where the pace is set by political rather than intellectual constraints. She has survived six years of this by developing a finely calibrated patience for official timelines that runs alongside a private urgency that she maintains through the reconstruction work she does outside formal Nexus hours.

She is not affiliated with any council faction in the sense of having made explicit commitments. The Culmination Faction funds her project and she has attended their informational events. She would accept resources from the Expansion Faction if they offered them with fewer strings than the Culmination arrangement currently carries. She is primarily interested in answering the Hypertext question, and faction affiliation is instrumental to that rather than primary.
