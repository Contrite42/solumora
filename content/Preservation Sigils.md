[[Preservation Sigils]] are low-to-mid complexity spell patterns used to slow degradation of food, materials, and sensitive equipment.

They are a logistics multiplier: extending viability windows so routes can run longer and inventories can absorb delay without total loss.

Most preservation work is routine and repetitive, which is why it is often overlooked in strategic summaries despite high practical value.
