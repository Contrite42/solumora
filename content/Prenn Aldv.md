**Prenn Aldv** is fifty-five years old and has farmed the same land in the agricultural belt of [[Auralis]] his entire life. He assessed [[Control Tier|T0]] at nine. He remembers the Luminant who administered the ceremony. He does not remember what the Luminant said, only the temperature of the room and the way his father's hands looked on his knees while the ceremony was being concluded.

The [[Ascendant Path]]'s official position on T0s — that they remain fully part of the Path, that contribution through witness and contemplation is a genuine form of advancement, that the journey does not require casting to be meaningful — was explained to him in the ceremony and has been repeated to him at intervals since. He has attended the Path's ceremonies his entire life. He finds them meaningful in the way that the rhythms of his life are meaningful: not as formal doctrine but as the structure that shapes his year.

What he does not think about, because he has not found it useful to think about, is the gap between what the Path says about T0s and how he has been treated for fifty-five years. The gap is real. It has expressed itself in small and consistent ways across a lifetime. He has found, over fifty-five years, that the most practical approach is to be good at the things he is good at and to let the gap be someone else's problem to articulate.

He is good at farming. The holding produces reliably. His neighbors ask his advice about soil conditions and planting timing, which has nothing to do with Flux and everything to do with knowledge accumulated over decades. He gives the advice honestly. He does not put much stock in the fact that they ask someone else when the question involves casting.

His daughter assessed T4 at eleven and left for Solhaven at sixteen. They write twice a year. He is proud of her and finds this uncomplicated.
