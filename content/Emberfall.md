**Emberfall** is [[Auralis]]'s primary interior city, situated in the warm lowland basin at the continent's southern mid-section, several days' travel east of [[Solhaven]]. Where the capital is a coastal trade hub, Emberfall is an agricultural and [[Flux]] research center — the place where Auralis does its quieter, longer-term work away from the political pressure of the Crestward.

## The City

Emberfall's population sits around three hundred thousand — roughly half the size of [[Solhaven]] but with a higher concentration of academic and research institutions per resident than anywhere else in [[Solumora]]. The city is built across the lowland basin's flat terrain: wide streets, large institutional buildings, the kind of horizontal sprawl that prioritizes function over display. It is warm year-round, occasionally humid, and the smell of the surrounding agricultural basin gives way, toward the city center, to the particular combination of chemical compounds and active [[Sigils]] that anyone who has spent time in research districts finds unmistakable.

The city's culture is academic in ways that produce a specific social environment. Emberfall is more interested in what you know than who you know, tolerant of eccentricity that comes packaged with demonstrated competence, and sharply intolerant of competence claims that cannot be demonstrated. High-tier casters here are not celebrated the way they are in the capital — they are assessed. A [[Control Tier|T6]] caster in Emberfall's research community is measured by their publication record and their experimental results, not by the ambient awareness of their Flux signature.

## The Research Institutions

Emberfall hosts three of [[Auralis|Auralis's]] major [[Flux Users|Scholar]] institutions, the largest concentration of formal magical research in Solumora. The oldest, the Emberhall, has been operating for over two hundred years and its archives contain the most complete collection of pre-modern sigil fragments outside the desert itself. The other two — the Institute for Applied Disciplines and the Reach Academy — are younger, more politically connected, and more directly useful to the council's long-term goals.

The Reach Academy is the institution [[Drest]] funds most aggressively. Its stated purpose is the study of extreme-range [[Flux]] effects. Its actual focus, understood by everyone who works there and never formally documented, is the investigation of whatever the desert civilization was doing with [[Flux]] at levels the current system cannot replicate. The Academy trains desert expedition members, processes recovered fragments, and provides the academic framework that connects Culmination Faction ambitions to legitimate scholarship. It is also where [[Rett Soln]] spends his off-season months, and where the material from his crossings arrives for analysis.

[[Sera Keln]] is Emberfall's most senior academic figure currently sitting on [[The Council of Auralis]]. Her relationship with the Emberhall is not entirely smooth — her council publications have qualified several findings the Culmination Faction wanted confirmed, and the research community watches the gap between what she reports academically and what she presents politically with careful attention.

## Relevance to the Council's Goals

Emberfall is where most of the artifact fragments recovered from [[Equatorial Desert|Desert Zakros]] expeditions are eventually brought for analysis. The city has developed significant expertise in working with damaged or partial [[Sigils|sigil]] inscriptions, and its Scholars have made more progress on the pre-modern script than anyone outside the desert itself.

Progress is slow. The script remains substantially unreadable. The council funds more expeditions anyway.
