Mythic spells require [[Control Tier]] T9 and can only be cast by Conduits — approximately 500 individuals across all of [[Solumora]]. The kingdom classification systems do not formally rate T9; the scale was not built to contain what happens at this level. Mythic spells are documented because Scholars believe they must be, not because anyone expects them to be used. Most people will live their entire lives without meeting someone capable of casting here.
