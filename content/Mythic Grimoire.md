_Rewrite spell - changes fundamental Flux relationship between two people_

The caster inscribes a sigil connecting two individuals through their Flux signatures. Upon activation, the target's Flux ceiling is permanently reduced to match or become subordinate to the reference individual. The effect is irreversible and affects only Control Tier capacity, not raw casting ability. Historical use suggests this spell was primary enforcement mechanism in early Council disputes.
