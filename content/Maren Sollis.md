_(Council Member, Auralis — Cassia's Faction)_

Maren Sollis is seventy-eight years old and has been in [[Solhaven]]'s political rooms for longer than most of the current council has been alive. She served under [[Cassia]]'s mother, transferred her loyalty without hesitation to the infant who survived her, and has spent twenty-three years doing the slow, unglamorous work of keeping that infant alive in a room full of people who find her useful but not important.

She is, by most measures, the least frightening person on [[The Council of Auralis]]. This is her primary political asset.

## Character

Maren operates entirely through kindness. Not performed kindness — genuine kindness, the kind that has been consistent for fifty years across every political climate and every shift in who holds power. She remembers birthdays. She asks after families. She sends notes when people are unwell. She has attended more funerals than anyone else on the council and cried at most of them.

The result of fifty years of this is that she knows everyone, everyone likes her, and almost nobody thinks of her as a threat until something has already happened. She has no significant [[Flux]] ability and has never needed it. She has never raised her voice in a council chamber. She has, on four separate occasions, prevented actions that would have materially damaged [[Cassia]]'s position without anyone being able to identify exactly how.

## Relationship with Cassia

Maren loved [[Cassia]]'s mother. The transfer of that loyalty to the daughter was not a political calculation — it happened the way things happen when a person you love dies and leaves something small and alive behind that needs protecting.

She does not treat Cassia as fragile. She treats her as someone who has been in a difficult situation for a very long time and deserves better, which is a different thing entirely. Of the three council members who back Cassia, Maren is the one whose support costs nothing to receive and means the most.

She is also the only person on the council who has ever told Cassia directly that she is kinder than [[Auralis]] deserves, and meant it as a compliment.

## On the Council

Maren's open support for [[Cassia]] draws more social cost than political cost — she is too well-liked to be punished directly and too old to be threatened with future consequences. [[Castor Helme]] finds her insufferable. She finds him transparent. They have maintained a professionally cordial relationship for eleven years, which is one of the more impressive political achievements in recent Auralis history.

Her relationship with [[Drest]] is warmer and more complicated. She knew him before any of this. She remembers who he was when [[Cassia]]'s mother was alive, which means she also remembers the distance between that person and who he became. She has never said this to him. She thinks about it.
