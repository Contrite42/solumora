Legendary spells require [[Control Tier]] T7–T8. At this level there are fewer than 10,000 people in all of [[Solumora]] capable of casting them. These spells do not circulate — they are held in restricted vaults, copied only under institutional oversight, and in some cases their existence is not publicly acknowledged. Channelers who can operate here is not simply powerful; they are a rare enough presence that both [[Terravelle]] and [[Auralis]] have quiet policies for how to handle them.
