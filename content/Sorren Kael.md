**Sorren Kael** is fifty-six years old, a senior Luminant with the [[Ascendant Path]] chapter in [[Hedun]], and has been in this posting for twenty years. Before Hedun he served in two other chapters — a smaller city, then a larger one — and before that he spent seven years in [[Solhaven]]'s senior academic program following his [[Control Tier|T4]] assessment.

The [[Ascendant Path]] in Hedun operates differently than elsewhere. The city's culture is horizontal in ways that Solhaven is not — tier indifference in public spaces, the particular pragmatism of a place built on hospitality and discretion. The chapter's work reflects this: less ceremonial, more individual. Sorren has adapted over twenty years to the point where he no longer thinks of this as adaptation. He finds the one-on-one practice more interesting than he found the formal ceremonial role, and he is better at it.

His T4 casting is used in the counseling work in ways the Path's formal curriculum does not fully account for — diagnostic assessment, emotional state reading at the margins of what Flux permits, the occasional direct intervention that is not strictly within the chapter's documented service scope but that the person in front of him needs. He has never been formally reviewed for this.

He keeps ceremony records as required by the chapter. He also keeps a separate private journal — not case files in any formal sense, but observations from the sessions that have stayed with him. Specifically the ones where the Path's framework, which he believes in and considers genuinely useful, did not fit what the person in front of him needed. He has been keeping this journal for twelve years. He is not sure what it is for.
