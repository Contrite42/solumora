# People of Solumora

This hub indexes named characters across [[Solumora]] — the working population who make the world's systems function, and the specific individuals whose lives intersect with the world's larger tensions.

Characters are organized by location and role. Most are cross-referenced across multiple categories. For characters with their own written works, see [[WrittenWorks]].

---

## Principal Characters

The people at the center of the world's main threads.

**[[Cassia]]** — Queen of Auralis in name; managed by a council that placed her on the throne. Politically constrained, personally capable, increasingly difficult to keep managed.

**[[Eddan Voss]]** — King of Terravelle. Pragmatist. Governing a kingdom being slowly pressured by Auralis without finding a clean public response.

**[[Drest]]** — Senior Culmination Faction member on the Council of Auralis. The long project. Believes the ancient civilization's achievement is recoverable.

**[[Sera Voss]]** — Head of the Voss family and [[The Wolfpoint Scholarium|Scholarium]] administrator. Has discovered that [[Watts]] = ancient civilization, has not told anyone.

**[[Cael]]** — Grew up in Valdenmoor, high-tier caster approaching the ceiling of what he can understand about channeling cost. Has seen Cassia at a diplomatic event.
