**Osvan Drellt** is forty-three years old and runs the water management operations for a mid-sized agricultural settlement in [[Terravelle]]'s interior, two weeks' travel from [[Valdenmoor]] and further still from anywhere the guild houses have regular presence. [[Control Tier|T4]]. He assessed at nine. The traveling assessor brought a second assessor back through the settlement two months later to confirm. The result has been his to manage ever since.

He has received six formal recruitment approaches over the past twenty years: two from guild houses in Valdenmoor, one from a scholarship institution in Ashford, one from a Terravelle military adjutant who came through the settlement on a survey mission and noted his tier in the official report, one from an agricultural research collective he has never heard of before or since, and one, four years ago, from someone who did not fully identify their institutional affiliation but was clearly well-resourced. He declined all six. The letters he drafts in response are polite and brief.

He is genuinely suited to the water management work. It requires T3-range casting for standard channel maintenance and his T4 allows him to do work that would otherwise require two practitioners, which in a settlement this remote matters practically. He understands the local terrain and seasonal patterns with the precision of someone who has made consequential decisions based on them for fifteen years.

The settlement has four hundred residents. They know his tier. They also know he is the person who kept the channels running during the drought three years ago when the southern approach failed. These two facts coexist without hierarchy.

What he has found: declining external attention is easy when you know what you are staying for.
