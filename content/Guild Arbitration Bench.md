The Guild Arbitration Bench is Terravelle's standing inter-guild dispute body for contract breaches, route interference, and certification conflicts.

It exists to end arguments before they break logistics.

## Funding

The Bench is funded by filing fees and charter maintenance contributions from participating [[Trade Guilds]] councils.

## Recruitment

Arbitrators are usually retired assessors, senior documentation clerks, and logistics officers with a record of neutral rulings.

## Rules

- evidence package required before hearing slot assignment
- emergency orders valid for one season unless renewed
- rulings logged and searchable for precedent

## Opposition

Parties that profit from ambiguous contracts try to route work around the Bench, especially in cross-border grimoire traffic where [[Cross-Border Grimoire Trade]] and [[Grimoire Rights and Restrictions]] collide.

## What It Trades In

It trades in enforceability, precedent, and lower-cost conflict resolution.
