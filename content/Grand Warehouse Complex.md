The [[Grand Warehouse Complex]] is the largest consolidated storage and transfer zone in [[Solhaven]]'s harbor logistics belt.

It handles bonded cargo staging, customs-hold inventory, and timed release to district distributors. Most high-volume maritime loads pass through at least one Complex checkpoint before final routing.

The site is known for strict record discipline and high dependence on practical low-tier workflow competence.
