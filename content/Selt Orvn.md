**Selt Orvn** is sixty-one years old and has run the same supply depot in [[Halveth]] for twenty-three years. [[Control Tier|T2]]. The depot handles outfitting for expeditions heading into [[The Bone Sea]] and the surrounding territory: food stocks, equipment, Flux-preservation supplies for items that need to survive months in demanding conditions, and the organizational documentation that expedition leaders use to track what they took and what came back.

He came to Halveth at thirty-eight from a provisioning career in [[Ashford]], following an offer from the previous depot owner that paid more than his Ashford work and required moving somewhere he found interesting. He has found Halveth interesting for twenty-three years. He does not regret the decision.

The depot work has made him the person in Halveth with the most complete working knowledge of what expeditions actually need versus what they think they need. He has seen expedition leaders arrive with careful lists that missed three critical items and leave with adjustments that saved the operation, and he has seen expedition leaders dismiss his suggestions and return — those who returned — knowing he was right. He does not say this when they return. He asks what they need for the next run.

He has met most of the people who pass through Halveth regularly. [[Wren]] comes through the depot two or three times a year. He has been doing business with Wren for eighteen years and has formed the conclusion that Wren is either the most consistently fortunate person he has ever met or is operating with information he does not have access to. He has not asked. The business is reliable, the payment is prompt, and Halveth has taught him that some questions are better left for someone else to answer.

His T2 casting is used for preservation work — keeping stocks viable over long storage periods, maintaining the integrity of Flux-sensitive equipment. Routine application, exactly matched to the work.

## Operational Profile Update (2026-03-08)
- Job routine: Runs depot allocation windows, checks expedition manifests, and stages survival stock before corridor departures.
- Non-public fact: Keeps a private count of teams he advised against departure that launched anyway under outside pressure.
- Institutional tie: Coordinates stock certification with Halveth Cooperative procurement and crossing guide rosters.
- Practical stakes: If he is seen as obstructing departures, council contractors can bypass his depot and strip his leverage.
