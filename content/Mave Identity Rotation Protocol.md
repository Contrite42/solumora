[[Mave]] sustains influence in [[Solhaven]] through identity rotation rather than territorial control. Her operational territory is social graph depth: households, guild circles, and council-adjacent service networks where trust compounds over time.

She maintains multiple active identity lanes with different visibility levels. One lane is high-legibility and professionally stable, used for predictable appointments and procurement. A second lane is lower-status and mobile, used for information collection and schedule mapping. A third lane is dormant reserve, activated only when a primary lane accumulates too much interpersonal memory.

Her replacement threshold is not suspicion but saturation. When an identity requires too many continuity repairs, she retires it and migrates objectives to another lane. This keeps each persona below "story debt" levels where acquaintances begin comparing inconsistencies.

Mave's daily discipline is conversational indexing. She records who expects what version of her behavior and distributes those expectations across lanes. The spell itself is effortless for her; the cognitive labor is continuity management at scale.

Her overlap protocol with [[Selvane]] is implicit. She avoids entering his high-value institutional corridors unless doing so grants access he cannot reach quickly. In exchange, he does not interfere with her social infiltration lanes unless they intersect active risk surfaces.
