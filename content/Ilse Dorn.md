_(Council Member, Auralis — Culmination Faction)_

**Ilse Dorn** is the most capable active caster on [[The Council of Auralis]], a [[Flux Users|Channeler]] operating at [[Control Tier]] T6 with a Force discipline that she has not deployed in a council session in eleven years. The fact that she could is what does the work. She has been a member of the Culmination Faction for fourteen years and has never needed to raise her voice.

## Character

Ilse is composed in the specific way of people who have stopped being surprised by things. She is not cold — she is simply a person who made her assessments long ago and has found little since to significantly revise them. She follows arguments to their conclusions faster than most people follow them at all, and has the particular patience of someone for whom waiting is not difficult because she is always doing something else while she waits.

She is forty-six. She came to the council through the [[Emberfall]] institutional network — she trained at the Institute for Applied Disciplines and spent a decade as a practicing Scholar before [[Drest]] offered her a seat. She took it. The transition from Scholar to council member was not difficult for her, because she had already noticed that the people making decisions about research were not the same people doing the research, and had decided she preferred to be the former.

## T6 and What It Means

Force discipline at T6 means Ilse can produce kinetic effects at a level that is not theoretical. The last time she used it in a professional context was not in [[Solhaven]] and is not documented in any council record. The council members who have been there longest have pieced together the general shape of it from scattered references and the particular care several people take not to raise certain topics in her presence.

She does not display this. She does not need to. The awareness operates on its own.

What she uses her tier for in practice is detection: Flux-sensitivity applications, reading the room in ways that go beyond social observation, the particular advantage of someone who can sense when someone nearby is channeling or about to. She has never mentioned this to the council. She does not feel obligated to.

## On the Culmination Goal

Ilse believes the ancient civilization in [[Equatorial Desert|Desert Zakros]] achieved something the current world cannot replicate. She believes this from the inscription work she did before the council, from the evidence base she can read more rigorously than most of the Culmination Faction can. She does not believe the goal as [[Drest]] frames it — the specifics of what the ruins might yield are less certain to her than his faith requires.

What she believes is that the question is worth pursuing. The distinction matters to her. It does not materially affect her voting record.
