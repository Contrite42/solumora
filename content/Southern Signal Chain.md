The Southern Signal Chain is a coordinated message-relay institution linking major Auralis corridors between [[Solhaven]], [[Hedun]], and [[Emberfall]].

It moves instructions faster than cargo and reduces how often offices make decisions on stale information.

## Funding

Funding comes from council dispatch budgets, merchant subscriptions, and premium emergency-message fees.

## Recruitment

The Chain hires relay scribes, tower operators, and low-tier signal teams trained for strict message formatting and verification discipline.

## Rules

- every priority message uses two-channel confirmation
- tactical alerts expire unless actively renewed
- chain operators do not edit content, only classify and relay

## Opposition

Any actor profiting from delay tries to jam, spoof, or buy signal priority. Rival dispatch networks in the north, including the [[Northern Relay Office Consortium]], are commercial competitors.

## What It Trades In

It trades in timing advantage and decision coherence across the southern kingdom.
