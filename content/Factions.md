Factions in [[Solumora]] are institutions that move work, risk, and authority between people who do not fully trust each other. They fund routes, certify grimoires, hire protection, and hold records long enough for accountability to survive a bad season.

Most people do not interact with a kingdom directly. They interact with a faction office, a guild chapter, a registry desk, or a courier coordinator.

## Terravelle Institutions

- [[Maren Ledger Syndicate]] - freight accounting and route credit on the [[Maren Freight Chain]].
- [[Ashford Dock Compact]] - berth control, dock security, and cargo-loss adjudication in [[Ashford]].
- [[Northern Relay Office Consortium]] - packet copying and dispatch routing between [[Greyveil]] and [[Wolfpoint]].
- [[Charter Road Wardens]] - contracted escort and road discipline on the [[Ashford-Halveth Courier Road]].
- [[Guild Arbitration Bench]] - binding dispute resolution between chartered trade bodies in [[Terravelle]].
- [[Covenant Relief Caravan]] - rural emergency supply movement tied to the [[Covenant of Measure]].

## Auralis Institutions

- [[Solhaven Elevation Works Office]] - terrace infrastructure and maintenance scheduling in [[Solhaven]].
- [[Hedun Quiet Contract Office]] - discreet legal brokerage for sensitive service contracts in [[Hedun]].
- [[Crestward Intake Bureau]] - talent intake and placement pipeline around [[The Council of Auralis]].
- [[Emberfall Expedition Registry]] - permits, incident logs, and sponsor bonds for desert work out of [[Emberfall]].
- [[Basin Grain Allocation Board]] - food reserve and district allocation control across the [[Auralis]] basin.
- [[Southern Signal Chain]] - high-priority coastal message routing between major southern cities.

## Existing Power Blocs

These institutions operate alongside established groups with broader political or religious authority, including [[Expansion Faction]], [[The Culmination Faction]], [[Ascendant Path]], [[Trade Guilds]], and [[The Advancement Corps]].

## What Factions Trade

The practical exchanges are consistent across both kingdoms:

- information certainty
- grimoire access and authentication
- route priority and protection
- labor placement and service bonds

The names and justifications differ by kingdom. The mechanics are similar.
