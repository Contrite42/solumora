# Guild Regulations

Guild regulations in Solumora govern trade practices, quality standards, licensing requirements, and dispute resolution procedures administered by merchant and craft guilds in both [[Terravelle]] and [[Auralis]].

These regulations are not static. They evolve in response to economic conditions, diplomatic pressure, and the strategic priorities of crown authorities who retain ultimate authority over guild policy while delegating operational implementation.

Changes to guild regulations often signal broader policy shifts before formal announcements, making them useful indicators for merchants who need to anticipate rather than merely react to new requirements.
