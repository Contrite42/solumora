[[Heat]] is the thermal discipline domain in practical Flux usage: managing temperature transfer, insulation behavior, and controlled thermal gradients.

Most common-tier heat work is defensive or logistical rather than offensive, including shelter conditioning, transport protection, and safe work environments.

Heat applications are among the most common route-support tools in desert and maritime operations.
