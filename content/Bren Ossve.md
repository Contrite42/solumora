**Bren Ossve** is sixty-one years old and lives on the land where she was born, in the upland farming country between [[Greyveil]] and the northern range.

She tested [[Control Tier|T7]] at fourteen. The regional guild sent assessors three more times over the following decade, as if expecting the number to have been an error or expecting her to have changed her mind about the farm. She cooperated with each assessment. She did not change her mind.

What she does with the T7 is mostly healing. The surrounding settlements within a day's travel know where she lives and what she can do, and people come when they have something that the standard practitioners cannot resolve. She handles these cases with the same approach she brings to the farm work: what does the situation require, what of what she has will address it, and no more than that. The [[Covenant of Measure]] would provide a framework for this way of working if she used it as a framework, which she does not; she uses it the way most rural Terravelle people use it, as background water, the way the things you were raised with become the way things are.

In forty years of practice she has had one case that required her full range. The patient was a young man from the eastern holdings with a structural collapse in the Flux pathways that the formal medical chapter in Greyveil had assessed as unresolvable. She spent three days and told no one afterward exactly what the three days involved. The young man recovered. He is now forty and farms with his family and she sees him twice a year at the seasonal market.

The guild offered her a teaching position in Greyveil when she was thirty-two. She declined. They have not asked again.
