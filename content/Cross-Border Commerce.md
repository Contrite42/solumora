# Cross-Border Commerce

Cross-border commerce between [[Terravelle]] and [[Auralis]] encompasses the full range of legitimate trade activity conducted through official channels: goods exchange, contract fulfillment, documentation processing, and relationship management across kingdom boundaries.

Its practical operation depends on established networks connecting merchants, guild representatives, and administrative contacts on both sides. Success requires procedural knowledge, relationship maintenance, and the ability to adapt to shifting regulatory standards without losing operational continuity.

The volume and character of cross-border commerce reflects broader diplomatic and economic conditions between the kingdoms, making trade patterns useful indicators of political climate even when policy changes have not been formally announced.
