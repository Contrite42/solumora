[[Flux]] is a renewable dimensional energy that permeates all of [[Solumora]]. Though ever-present, it cannot be freely harnessed by just anyone. Individuals serve as conduits for Flux, but each person's capacity is finite and unique to them — and does not reliably follow bloodlines, wealth, or social standing.

The [[Sigils]] used to cast [[Spells]] are the precise frameworks that shape Flux into defined form. A caster's innate or trained ability — measured by [[Control Tier]] — determines how much Flux they can channel safely. Some individuals may never reach the threshold to cast even the simplest [[Spells|spells]], while others can channel amounts that defy normal understanding. The balance of power in [[Solumora]] is therefore as much about individual attunement to Flux as it is about the precision of the spellcraft.

Flux is abundant. Its use is defined entirely by the person wielding it.

Those who work with Flux are organized into a broad hierarchy detailed on the [[Flux Users]] page, ranging from [[Flux Users|Scribes]] who copy and distribute [[Grimoires]] to [[Flux Users|Conduits]] who operate beyond what the kingdom classification systems can meaningfully measure.
