The Ashford Dock Compact is a port-side institution in [[Ashford]] that coordinates berth windows, bonded handling, and dock-floor protection contracts.

It is not city government. It is the group that keeps port volume moving when city offices are behind.

## Funding

The Compact is funded by berth fees, bonded-storage surcharges, and private protection retainers from shippers using eastern export lanes.

## Recruitment

Recruitment comes from dock crews, manifest clerks, low-tier preservation specialists, and former harbor guards who know how cargo disputes escalate.

## Rules

- manifests filed before berth assignment
- exposed grimoires require sealed handling and verification reference
- protection contracts are neutral to guild politics while cargo is on dock ground

## Opposition

The Compact regularly clashes with smuggling intermediaries, especially groups aligned with [[Black Market Grimoire Circulation]] and opportunists trying to bypass [[Grimoire Authentication]] requirements.

## What It Trades In

It trades in protection, handling speed, and predictability under pressure.
