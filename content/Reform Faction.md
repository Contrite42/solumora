The [[Reform Faction]] is a broad label for council-adjacent actors in [[Auralis]] who favor procedural restraint, transparency, and slower escalation in cross-border policy.

It is less structurally coherent than the [[Expansion Faction]] and often operates through temporary voting alignments rather than stable command discipline.

Its influence rises when administrative risk is high and falls when security narratives dominate.
