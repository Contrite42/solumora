The [[Bone Road Consortium]] is a logistics partnership that coordinates heavy overland movement on brittle, fracture-prone routes where ordinary freight practices fail.

Its operators specialize in convoy timing, load balancing, and contingency rerouting under poor terrain conditions. The consortium's value is reliability under conditions where speed is less important than completion.

In policy terms, it functions as a contracting intermediary between local route owners, merchant houses, and expedition buyers.
