[[Expedition Supplies]] are the minimum durable goods required for multi-day operations outside stable settlement infrastructure.

Standard lists include water containment, preserved food, shelter materials, sigil-safe writing surfaces, and replacement tool stock. For Zakros-adjacent work, load planning is based on failure tolerance rather than nominal consumption.

Supply quality matters more than quantity when replacement access is uncertain.
