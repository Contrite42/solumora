**Essa Rolt** is forty-one years old and manages the household staff for a mid-Crestward residence in [[Solhaven]]. [[Control Tier|T1]]. She has held this position for eight years, having spent her twenties in lower-terrace domestic service and worked her way to the management layer through demonstrated competence and the accumulated trust that household management requires.

The household's principal is [[Selvane]]. Essa manages a staff of seven, coordinates with external suppliers, and handles the administrative operations of a Crestward household — the scheduling, documentation, the complex social calendar that a Crestward residence requires. She has worked for Selvane for eight years and considers herself good at her job.

She has a set of observations she has never organized into a coherent account because the observations do not cohere. The principal does not sleep, or does not appear to sleep, though the bedroom is occupied and the staff instructions around it are normal. The principal has never, in eight years, been sick. The principal's conversational retention is unusual — not perfect recall, which exists, but something that includes recalling the specific texture of interactions from years ago with a precision that suggests a different relationship to time than the staff experiences. None of this is explicable as abnormality in the usual sense. Each observation has a potential explanation. The pattern of observations together is what Essa cannot explain.

She does not discuss this with the other staff. The household runs well. She has been treated fairly. She has no desire to leave.
