[[Soul]] refers to identity-level Flux interaction: effects that target continuity of personhood rather than body state or surface cognition.

Soul-adjacent work is rare, expensive, and heavily constrained by both technical uncertainty and ethical concern. Most practitioners encounter it only through historical records, restricted research notes, or incident reports.

In common discourse, "soul" terminology often marks uncertainty as much as knowledge.
