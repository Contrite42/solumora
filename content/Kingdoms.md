[[Solumora]] is divided into two great kingdoms, separated by the impassable expanse of [[Equatorial Desert|Desert Zakros]].

[[Terravelle]] occupies the northern belt — defined by pragmatism, efficiency, and a functional relationship with [[Flux]]. Magic is a trade skill there. Restraint is a virtue. [[Auralis]] holds the southern reaches, driven by ambition, magical innovation, and a belief that mastering Flux is the highest pursuit a person can dedicate their life to.

The two kingdoms share a language, a currency ([[Watts]]), and the same [[Flux Users|hierarchy of Flux users]], but little else. Their philosophies are fundamentally opposed, their rulers are patient adversaries, and the conditions for open conflict are slowly being met.
