**Ressa Dolt** is thirty-eight years old and works as a [[Birth Runes|Birth Rune]] inscriber at an [[Ascendant Path]] administrative chapter in [[Solhaven]]'s middle terraces. [[Control Tier|T2]]. She was trained in the inscription craft at twenty-two, certified through the Path's internal accreditation process at twenty-five, and has been inscribing runes since: attending births across the chapter's district, performing the inscription within the required window, recording the registration, filing the documentation with both the Path's chapter records and [[Auralis]]'s civic registry.

## Appearance

Ressa is thirty-eight with the calm, methodical bearing of someone who has performed the same precise ceremonial work for thirteen years without error. Her dark brown hair is worn in a simple braided style appropriate for Path administrative staff, and she dresses in the formal robes of an inscriber — deep indigo with silver trim that marks her certification level — always immaculate and properly arranged. The robes are both ceremonial garb and practical work clothing, designed to move easily during inscription work and project the appropriate gravity for the foundational administrative act of Auralis citizenship.

Her hands are her most notable feature: remarkably steady from years of precision inscription work, with pronounced calluses on her right hand from holding inscription tools in the exact same grip for over two thousand runes. She moves with deliberate care, the physical habit of someone whose work cannot be corrected after the fact, and carries a leather case with her inscription kit wherever she goes — the tools cleaned and maintained with the same meticulous attention she brings to every rune.

Her expression is professionally serene, the cultivated calm of someone who attends births in all circumstances and must project confidence regardless of the situation. She has a low, clear voice trained for speaking the inscription blessing, and the slight fatigue visible around her eyes speaks to years of irregular hours responding to births across her chapter's district at all times of day and night. When reviewing registration documentation she reaches automatically for reading glasses, though she doesn't wear them during inscription work itself — the muscle memory of precision focus making artificial assistance unnecessary for the work that matters most.

## Work

She inscribes approximately two hundred runes per year. She has inscribed over two thousand in her career. Each one is permanent, tied to the individual in a way that cannot be transferred or altered, and connects the individual to the [[Watts]] ledger for their lifetime. This is the foundational administrative act of Auralis citizenship and she performs it with the competence of someone for whom foundational administrative acts are the work.

She does not think often about the metaphysical claims the [[Ascendant Path]] makes about Birth Runes — the doctrine of connection to the Path of ascendance, the theology of the Flux-bond as spiritual as well as practical. She is aware of these claims. She is a member of the Path in the same way she is a citizen of Auralis: because she was born into it, because it structures the world she operates in, and because she has not found a compelling reason to organize her life around questioning its foundations.

What she thinks about: the quality of the inscription work. A rune inscribed incorrectly can create documentation problems for the individual for decades. She has never inscribed one incorrectly. She expects to maintain this.
