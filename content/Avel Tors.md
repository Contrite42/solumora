**Avel Tors** is thirty-three years old and runs a small workshop in [[Solhaven]]'s middle terraces producing decorative metalwork for the bluff district's hospitality establishments and the Crestward's private residences. [[Control Tier|T1]]. The workshop has three employees and produces adequate income. She has been in business for seven years and expects to remain in business for another twenty.

She has met [[Mave]] on four occasions. Three were professional — commissions from the establishment Mave uses as a base of operations in Solhaven, delivered with Mave present for the final review. One was social, at a gathering of middle-terrace craftspeople she attends quarterly. She knows Mave as a person of wealth and uncertain prior occupation who has been in Solhaven for several years and who tends to ask precise questions and then listen to the answers with a focus that she has noticed is not universal in clients of Mave's apparent status.

She does not think about Mave between encounters. Mave is one of approximately a hundred people she knows by name and face in the city's commercial layer. The commissions have been paid promptly and the specifications have been clear. This is, in her working taxonomy, a good client.

The specific thing she could not explain if asked: when she delivered the third commission and Mave reviewed the work, she had the brief and completely inexplicable sense that the person across the table from her was extremely old. Not in any visible way. Just as a quality of the room. She mentioned this to no one. It does not come back to her unless something triggers the memory. She has not been able to identify what triggers it.
