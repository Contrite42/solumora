T0 and non-Flux outcomes are not a side note in [[Solumora]]. They are the center of the labor system.

A large share of the population enters work without high-tier casting prospects, and economic continuity depends on how well systems convert this majority into stable roles.

## Typical Entry Lanes

Most T0 and non-Flux entrants move into:

- logistics handling and route support;
- dock, market, and warehouse operations;
- recordkeeping and documentation support;
- maintenance, repair, and service labor;
- assistant roles in clinics, workshops, and guild offices.

These lanes are skill-heavy even when socially labeled "unskilled."

## Advantage Profile

T0 workers hold structural advantages in domains where consistency matters more than casting output:

- lower Flux-overstrain risk;
- predictable schedule tolerance;
- strong procedural reliability in repetitive systems;
- high value in mixed teams requiring stable handoff discipline.

Many institutions quietly depend on these traits while publicly rewarding tier spectacle.

## Common Constraints

Recurring constraints include:

- exclusion from jobs with formal tier minimums;
- slower advancement in prestige hierarchies;
- under-credentialing despite high practical competence;
- vulnerability to wage suppression in oversupplied labor markets.

Where wage protections are weak, T0 workers absorb system volatility first.

## Terravelle Outcomes

In [[Terravelle]], T0 entry is often mediated through guild and neighborhood networks. Progression is possible through reputation and reliability even without high-tier ability, but outcomes vary widely by district and mentor quality.

## Auralis Outcomes

In [[Auralis]], T0 entry is more legible but more rank-sensitive. Institutional tracks can provide stable placement, yet status ceilings are more visible and harder to bypass without sponsorship.

## Upward Movement Patterns

Upward movement usually comes from specialization, not tier change:

- becoming a trusted route coordinator;
- moving from labor to ledger authority;
- mastering a narrow technical support function;
- building cross-institution reliability that converts to bargaining power.

This movement is real but slower than Path-centered narratives suggest.

## Policy-Relevant Reality

Any education policy that treats T0 outcomes as fallback will underperform. Systems that deliberately train and credential T0-majority pathways produce stronger economic resilience and lower long-term inequality.
