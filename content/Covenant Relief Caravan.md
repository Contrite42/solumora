The Covenant Relief Caravan is a Terravellian mutual-aid logistics body connected to the [[Covenant of Measure]]. It moves food, medicine, and basic repair supplies into rural zones during shortage periods.

It is conservative by design: keep villages functional first, debate policy later.

## Funding

Funding comes from covenant tithes, guild relief pools, and fixed-price surplus purchases from valley producers during high-yield seasons.

## Recruitment

Recruitment focuses on local drivers, quartermasters, and practical casters trained for preservation and emergency stabilization work.

## Rules

- distribution starts from documented household counts
- no convoy may sell above posted emergency margin
- aid routes stay neutral during guild and council disputes

## Opposition

The Caravan is opposed by predatory lenders and market actors who gain leverage from scarcity, especially when rural households have no short-term access to [[Watts]] credit.

## What It Trades In

It trades in continuity: one more week of food, one more month of transport access, one less forced migration to city edges.
