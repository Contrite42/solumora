Toren Vass is field quartermaster for [[Crestward Field Company]], responsible for expedition load architecture, replacement-stock ratios, and cache viability across council-backed [[Ancient Ruins]] missions.

He is [[Control Tier|Tier 3]], 37 years old. His specialty is preventing missions from collapsing due to logistics debt that looked acceptable on paper.

## Work Profile

Toren handles the unglamorous failure layer of expedition culture:

- water and thermal-management reserve calculations  
- grimoire durability allocation under wind-particulate stress  
- replacement tool staging by route phase  
- rejection of sponsor substitutions that reduce component reliability

He tracks depletion curves aggressively. If a team consumes reserve stock faster than modeled, he triggers objective reduction before personnel become the reserve.

## Operating Principle

His standard line: "A mission that spends its margin in week one is already in retrieval mode."

This position makes him unpopular with teams that prefer optimistic planning and with sponsors who frame durability constraints as low-confidence behavior.

## Network

Toren's work depends on coordination with [[Expedition Supplies]] pipelines, guide timing from [[Halveth Guide Bureau]], and incident trend data from [[Zakros Loss Table]]. He treats procurement, route windows, and casualty patterns as one system.

He has standing relationships with depot operators near [[Halveth]] and with southern staging staff in [[Emberfall]], and he requires signed variance records whenever any layer overrides his minimum stock recommendations.

## Why He Matters

Expedition stories center on discoveries and losses. Toren's contribution is mostly invisible when it works: teams run out of ambition before they run out of water.

That ordering keeps people alive.
