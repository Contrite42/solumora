# Border Guards

Border guards in Solumora are stationed at designated crossing points between [[Terravelle]] and [[Auralis]], responsible for traveler documentation, cargo inspection, and maintaining security protocols at kingdom boundaries.

Their role combines regulatory enforcement with intelligence gathering. Guard posts log traveler movements, cargo types, and crossing patterns, creating records that inform both immediate security decisions and longer-term policy analysis.

Individual guards work under local command structures but answer to crown authority for protocol compliance and reporting requirements. Their effectiveness depends on procedural consistency, accurate documentation, and the ability to distinguish routine traffic from patterns that warrant escalation.
