The Charter Road Wardens are licensed escort companies operating on the [[Ashford-Halveth Courier Road]] and adjacent desert-edge approach roads.

They are hired to prevent delays from becoming losses.

## Funding

Wardens are funded through escort retainers, convoy fees, and contract pools underwritten by Terravellian merchant houses with regular exposure to [[Halveth]] traffic.

## Recruitment

Most recruits are former route guards, courier outriders, and expedition support staff from Ashford and Halveth.

## Rules

- no private detours once a convoy lock is signed
- medical and dispatch-critical cargo take escort priority
- all Rift contact events reported within one watch cycle

## Opposition

Wardens absorb pressure from raider crews, panic-routing during [[Rift Incursions]], and contract conflicts with operators who treat schedule as optional.

## What It Trades In

It trades in controlled movement, survival odds, and route discipline under hostile conditions.
