**The Council of Auralis — Full Membership**

The twenty members of [[The Council of Auralis]], organized by faction. Seats are not elected and not hereditary by rule — in practice, vacancies are filled by council consensus, which means the body has been self-selecting for two decades and reflects [[Drest]]'s influence accordingly.

---

## Culmination Faction

_Eleven members. Organized around the recovery of the ancient civilization's work in [[Equatorial Desert|Desert Zakros]]. Led by [[Drest]]._

**[[Drest]]** — Archduke. Founding force of the current council. Loudest believer in the desert goal. Possibly [[Cassia]]'s father.

**[[Oren Vael]]** — The faction's primary financier. Funds expedition equipment through a network of merchant proxies. Has never been to the desert himself and has no intention of going.

**[[Sera Keln]]** — Senior Scholar from [[Emberfall]]. The council's most credible academic voice on the ruins research. Believes in the goal from evidence rather than faith, which makes her more dangerous than the true believers.

**[[Aldric Mourne]]** — Former military commander, now retired into council politics. Manages the logistics of desert expeditions with the same detachment he once managed supply lines. Does not ask what the expeditions are looking for.

**[[Tess Varun]]** — Young, recently appointed, Drest's most vocal echo. Repeats his positions with slightly less nuance and considerably more volume. The council's bellwether — watch which way she votes to know which way Drest is leaning.

**[[Hadren Cosse]]** — Handles the council's relationship with the Ascendant Path clergy in [[Solhaven]]. The theological justification for the Culmination goal runs through him. Believes sincerely. Is not above using that sincerity instrumentally.

**[[Pol Ashvane]]** — Quiet, precise, rarely speaks in full sessions. His influence operates through private conversation and strategic abstention. Three council decisions in the last five years went the way he wanted without him visibly pushing for any of them.

**[[Corda Lenn]]** — The faction's institutional memory. Has served since the council's formation under Drest. Knows where everything is buried, literally and otherwise. Old enough that people have started assuming she will retire. She has not.

**[[Veran Ostl]]** — Controls a significant portion of Auralis's eastern port trade. Supports the Culmination goal because Drest told him to and the arrangement has been profitable. Would switch factions if the math changed. Drest knows this and keeps the math from changing.

**[[Ilse Dorn]]** — The council's most accomplished active caster at T6. Rarely deploys this in sessions. The awareness that she could is sufficient.

**Mira Solv** — Manages the council's correspondence with [[Wolfpoint]] on Drest's behalf. Has not told Drest everything the Voss line has written back. Has not decided what to do with the parts she has withheld.

---

## Expansion Faction

_Six members. Organized around territorial growth, trade control, and strategic pressure on [[Terravelle]]. No single dominant leader — [[Castor Helme]] is the loudest voice._

**[[Castor Helme]]** — Theatrical, competent, dismissive of Cassia, quietly useful to everyone who underestimates him.

**[[Davan Rhyce]]** — The faction's strategic mind. Where Castor is loud, Davan is careful. He has been mapping Terravelle's eastern vulnerabilities for eleven years and considers himself close to something actionable.

**[[Orla Fest]]** — Controls Auralis's western shipping lanes. Supports expansion because her business model requires it. Not ideological. Entirely predictable, which Tovan Ashce considers her most useful quality.

**[[Brennan Solce]]** — Former border administrator. Knows the geography between the kingdoms better than anyone in the room. Has opinions about where a campaign would succeed and where it would not, and shares them only with people he has decided to trust, which is a short list.

**[[Yara Venn]]** — The youngest member of the Expansion Faction at thirty-one. Ambitious, impatient, occasionally sloppy. Castor is grooming her as a future voice. Tovan is watching her for the sloppiness.

**[[Celd Arren]]** — Old money, old family, old grievances with Terravelle that predate the current political situation by two generations. Does not need a strategic reason to support expansion. Has one anyway.

---

## Cassia's Faction

_Three members. No shared ideology beyond genuine loyalty to [[Cassia]] as a person._

**[[Maren Sollis]]** — Seventy-eight, politically tireless, operates through kindness. Has been protecting Cassia since she was four. The council's conscience, whether it wants one or not.

**[[Tovan Ashce]]** — Twenty-eight, carries four generations of institutional weight, thinks like someone three times his age. Supports Cassia through structural obstruction rather than open advocacy.

**[[Drest]]** — Listed here as well as Culmination because his support for Cassia is structurally real, however complicated its origins. The only member who appears in both columns. Neither faction fully trusts him for it.
