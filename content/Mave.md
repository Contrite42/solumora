Mave is the youngest [[High Demons|High Demon]] in [[Solumora]], somewhere under a century old and currently operating at the lower end of T3 climbing toward T4. She arrived with goals she had been developing since shortly after she came through the rift and has been executing on them with the particular intensity of someone who does not yet know how much time she has.

## Appearance

Mave presents as a young woman — late twenties by human appearance — with the kind of looks that register as striking rather than beautiful, all sharp angles and deliberate presentation. Her horns are significant: tall, forward-swept, and deeply impractical to conceal. She wears her hair elaborately as a matter of necessity and has developed a secondary cover story involving a disfiguring scar that the hair conceals, which satisfies casual curiosity without inviting closer examination.

She is aware this solution is imperfect. She is also aware that she has been operating on it for decades without it failing, which she takes as evidence of her own skill rather than luck. [[Selvane]] would disagree with this assessment.

## Discipline

Mave's spell consumes a person entirely — soul signature, identity, physical form — and becomes them. Not an imitation. Not a temporary mask. She is that person, completely and without residue, for as long as she chooses to hold the form. The original does not survive the process.

On paper, her overall [[Control Tier]] is low. In practice, this distinction is largely irrelevant to what she can do. It is the only spell she has. She has practiced nothing else since the moment she understood what she was capable of. A human caster at T3 reaching for this effect would find it effortful, inconsistent, and traceable. Mave finds it effortless in the way that breathing is effortless — not because it requires no Flux, but because she has never once had to think about how to do it. The execution is perfect. The ceiling is her imagination and her patience, not her capacity.

The practical limit is acquisition. She needs access to the person before she can become them, which requires her to be Mave long enough to get close. This is the part she finds interesting. Everything after is simply the spell.

She does not leave bodies in any conventional sense. There is nothing left to find.

## Goals

Mave came through the rift young and with the particular energy of someone who does not yet know what they want but is certain they want something. What she found in [[Solhaven]] was [[Selvane]]: older, purposeful, already moving toward something he had not named to her. She attached herself to his orbit and has stayed.

Her interest in the [[Ancient Ruins|ruins]] is genuine but derivative — she knows Selvane wants something from the Flux research the council produces, and she has decided that being present for whatever he finds is more interesting than pursuing her own direction independently. She has her own reasons for caring about a permanent connection between Solumora and the [[The Flux Dimension|Flux dimension]], reasons she has not examined closely enough to articulate. She does not need to understand her own motivations to act on them.

The identities she has consumed in [[Solhaven]] have given her the access that Selvane cannot easily build himself. Several people currently trusted by the council are Mave. Have been Mave for years. The people they replaced are not somewhere waiting to be found. She does not share everything she learns from these positions. He does not ask her to.

## Relationship with Selvane

[[Selvane]] was already in [[Solhaven]] when Mave arrived. She came knowing he was there. He ran disruptions against her early operations — a demonstration that he was aware of her and older and that she was operating in space he had prepared. She absorbed this and stayed, and he has not since made a serious effort to move her.

Their arrangement is not a partnership and not a conflict. She follows his general direction without being told what it is, which either means she has understood more than he intended or that she is simply moving in the same direction for her own reasons. He has not decided which. She finds his patience difficult to hold and his methods slow. He finds her reckless in ways he has decided to work around rather than address.
