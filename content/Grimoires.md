A Grimoire is a bound collection of [[Sigils]] — a reference work that allows any caster of sufficient [[Control Tier]] to access, reproduce, and cast [[Spells|spells]] they did not design themselves. Grimoires are the backbone of distributed [[Flux]] use across [[Solumora]]. Without them, most of the population would have access only to spells they personally developed or memorized.

## How Grimoires Work

Each [[Sigils|sigil]] in a grimoire is drawn with full precision. A caster who meets the [[Control Tier]] requirement for a given spell can activate it directly from the page, or copy it onto another surface for single use. The quality of the grimoire matters — a poorly copied sigil may misfire, underperform, or fail entirely.

[[Flux Users|Scribes]] are responsible for the production of most grimoires in circulation. High-quality Scribe work is commercially valuable and socially respected, particularly in [[Terravelle]].

## Grimoire Tiers

Grimoires are categorized by the rarity of the [[Spells|spells]] they contain, reflecting the [[Control Tier]] required to use them:

| Tier | Control Tier | Typical Holder |
|---|---|---|
| [[Common Grimoire]] | T1–T2 | Most households |
| [[Uncommon Grimoire]] | T3–T4 | Skilled [[Flux Users\|Formulists]] |
| [[Rare Grimoire]] | T5–T6 | [[Flux Users\|Channelers]], restricted |
| [[Legendary Grimoire]] | T7–T8 | Institutional holdings only |
| [[Mythic Grimoire]] | T9 | [[Flux Users\|Conduits]], documentation only |
| [[Pale Grimoire]] | T9 | Restricted scholarly record |

The full catalogue of documented spells across all tiers is maintained in the [[All Grimoire]].

## Cultural Attitudes

In [[Terravelle]], grimoires are treated as practical tools — functional, affordable at the Common tier, and widely distributed. High-tier grimoires exist but are held in institutional libraries rather than private hands.

In [[Auralis]], grimoires are also status objects. The tier of grimoire a household owns signals something about their position in society. [[Rare Grimoire|Rare]] and above texts circulate through [[Flux Users|Scholar]] networks, private patronage arrangements, and the occasional very expensive sale.
