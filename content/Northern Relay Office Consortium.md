The Northern Relay Office Consortium is a federation of dispatch houses that manages copy, handoff, and routing standards for traffic moving through [[Greyveil]] toward [[Wolfpoint]].

Its leverage comes from reliability, not force.

## Funding

The Consortium is funded by annual subscription contracts from Terravellian commercial houses and per-packet fees on long-haul relay traffic.

## Recruitment

It hires copy clerks, route planners, and relay riders from northern towns, with promotion tied to error rate over full winter cycles.

## Rules

- all priority packets are copied before departure
- each handoff receives timestamp and witness mark
- dispatch timing is published in arrival bands, not exact promises

## Opposition

The Consortium competes with private brokers selling unlogged speed and treats signal disruption from rival networks, including the [[Southern Signal Chain]], as a direct threat to its contract base.

## What It Trades In

It trades in information custody, continuity, and proof that a message was actually sent.
