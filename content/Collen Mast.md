**Collen Mast** is thirty-three years old and serves in an [[Auralis]] mobile border unit operating in the territory south of [[Ashford]]. [[Control Tier|T2]]. She enlisted at twenty, spent four years in a garrison posting in southern Auralis, and has been in mobile unit work for nine years. Mobile units handle situations that fixed border posts cannot: disputed jurisdiction cases, cross-territory incidents, cargo seizures that require both legal authority and the ability to catch up with the cargo. The work suits her in ways that garrison posting did not.

The political briefings from her unit's command have been different in the past year. She has been in long enough to have a baseline for what standard briefings sound like, and the language around northern territories and the Terravelle border has shifted in ways she reads accurately. The operational restrictions that would normally govern her unit's area of movement have been quietly relaxed. She has not been told why. She has made observations.

What she observes: more Terravelle commercial documentation moving through the territory she covers, with authentication patterns she finds slightly inconsistent with the documentation's stated origin. A higher frequency of requests from her command for specific route information rather than general area coverage. Two situations in the past six months where her unit's presence had a secondary effect of being visible to specific parties who, she noted afterward, had been the subject of recent intelligence requests.

She has not raised any of this with her command. Her command has not asked. She keeps accurate records of what her unit does and where and when. The records are part of her professional practice. They are also, she understands, the only protection she has if something goes wrong.
