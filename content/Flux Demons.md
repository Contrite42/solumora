Flux Demons are creatures native to a dimension adjacent to Solumora's own — a space of pure, dense [[Flux]] that exists alongside the physical world without intersecting it under normal conditions. They are not conjured, summoned, or created by spellwork. They arrive through rifts: tears in the boundary between dimensions that form naturally in areas of extreme Flux density.

The [[Equatorial Desert|Desert Zakros]] is the primary site of rift formation in the known world. The anomalous Flux concentrations around the ancient ruin sites create conditions favorable to boundary tears, and incursions have been occurring with increasing frequency as those sites are disturbed by [[Auralis]] expedition activity.

Flux Demons are not a single type of creature. Different rifts produce different forms, and the variety suggests a genuine native ecology in their dimension of origin — distinct species, distinct behaviors, distinct threat profiles. What they share is a Flux-dense biology, the capacity to survive and range freely once they cross into Solumora, and the complete absence of any natural limit on how long they can live.

## Entry and Survival

Rifts open without warning, typically in zones where ambient Flux density has spiked beyond a threshold the boundary can sustain. The creatures that emerge are disoriented briefly, then acclimate. Unlike the native [[Equatorial Fauna]], which evolved within Solumora's Flux gradient, Flux Demons carry their own internal Flux density — they do not require high-Flux environments to survive after crossing. Once through, they range freely.

There is no documented case of a [[Flux Demons|Flux Demon]] returning through a rift voluntarily. Whether they cannot, or simply do not, is unknown.

The dimension they originate from is documented separately — see [[The Flux Dimension]].

## Forms

Lower-order Flux Demons vary widely in appearance — some are animal-like in silhouette but wrong in proportion, material, or behavior, others have no earthly reference point at all. The common thread is a visible or detectable Flux signature that distinguishes them from native fauna even at a distance.

The apex form is the [[High Demons|High Demon]], which presents as fully humanoid. See that page for a full account.

## Flux Ability

Lower-order demons interact with Flux instinctively rather than through learned casting. They do not use [[Sigils]], [[Hypertext]], or [[Hand Signs]]. Their effects are raw, undirected, and consistent with low-to-mid [[Control Tier]] output. They are dangerous primarily through physical capability and the disrupting effect their Flux density has on active spells in their vicinity.

## Current Situation

Flux Demon incursions are concentrated at the southern edge of the [[Equatorial Desert]], where the desert meets the northern reaches of [[Auralis]] territory. The affected population is sparse — frontier settlements, trade routes, border communities — which has allowed the [[The Council of Auralis|council]] to treat the incursion rate as an acceptable ongoing cost rather than a crisis requiring intervention.

[[Terravelle]] is aware of the situation and considers it a border crisis. Formal escalation requires Auralis cooperation that has not been forthcoming.
