_(Council Member, Auralis — Expansion Faction)_

Castor Helme is the loudest member of the Expansion Faction on [[The Council of Auralis]] and the most visible obstacle between [[Cassia]] and anything resembling actual authority. He is not the most powerful person on the council. He is the most present, which in a body of twenty has proven to be nearly as effective.

He introduced himself to [[Cassia]] twice at her own coronation banquet. He has never acknowledged this.

## Character

Castor is theatrical in the specific way of people who have discovered that performance works and have never been given sufficient reason to stop. He is large in every sense — loud voice, broad gestures, opinions on everything delivered with the confidence of someone who has never been seriously wrong in their own estimation. He is charming in the way that makes people laugh and then feel obscurely that they should not have.

He is also, underneath the performance, genuinely competent. This is what makes him dangerous rather than merely irritating. The theatrics are not a cover for incompetence — they are a cover for the quieter work he does when nobody is watching him because they are too busy watching the performance.

His [[Flux]] ability sits at T2, which he does not advertise. What he uses it for, in the small and careful ways that T2 allows, is information. Charged [[Sigils]] on correspondence he handles. Faint detection work at the margins of social gatherings. Nothing that would be considered a violation if examined directly — everything calibrated to stay just inside the line.

## On the Council

Castor leads the Expansion Faction's public voice. He argues for territorial consolidation, trade route control, and the kind of strategic pressure on [[Terravelle]] that keeps the border situation quietly uncomfortable without tipping into open conflict — not because he objects to open conflict, but because he believes the timing is not yet right.

He and [[Drest]] have a functional alliance of convenience. Castor does not believe in the Culmination Faction's goal and considers the desert expedition funding a waste of resources that could be spent on expansion. Drest considers Castor useful, predictable, and not particularly interesting. They vote together when their interests align and against each other when they don't, and both of them understand the arrangement clearly.

## Relationship with Cassia

Castor is not cruel to [[Cassia]]. He is dismissive, which she finds worse. He treats her the way you treat a piece of furniture that has been in the room so long you have stopped seeing it — present, functional in a narrow sense, not something you consider when making decisions.

He has been surprised, on three occasions, to discover that she was paying more attention than he assumed. He has not updated his behavior in response to any of these occasions, which [[Tovan Ashce]] considers the most useful thing about him.

[[Maren Sollis]] finds him insufferable. He finds her irrelevant. They have maintained a professionally cordial relationship for eleven years, which is one of the more impressive political achievements in recent Auralis history.
