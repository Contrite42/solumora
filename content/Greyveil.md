**Greyveil** is a mid-sized city in the northern reaches of [[Terravelle]]'s settled belt, the last significant settlement before the terrain becomes too cold and open for comfortable habitation. It functions as the kingdom's northern waypoint — the place where travelers heading further north resupply, where correspondence to and from [[Wolfpoint]] passes through, and where the kingdom's awareness of what is happening in the far north is collected and occasionally forwarded to [[Valdenmoor]].

Greyveil is not strategically important by conventional measures. It has no significant port, no major river, and no natural resource that isn't available in more accessible locations. Its importance is almost entirely about position — it sits at the edge of the map, and things that come from beyond the map tend to pass through here.

## The Wolfpoint Connection

Most of what [[Terravelle]] officially knows about [[Wolfpoint]] arrived via Greyveil. The city has a small but established community of traders who make the northern run regularly, and a few individuals who have spent time in Wolfpoint itself and returned with working knowledge of [[Hypertext]] fragments. These individuals are not officially recognized as anything — [[Terravelle]] has no formal policy on Hypertext — but they are quietly aware of themselves as a category, and Greyveil is where they tend to cluster.

The community is informal and overlapping. Some are traders who encountered Hypertext incidentally during extended stays at Wolfpoint and came back with enough to use practically, without any theoretical framework for what they have. Others are more serious — people who have made the northern run specifically to study, who correspond with Wolfpoint on their own account, and who have begun applying Hypertext sequences to their own work in ways that are visible to anyone paying attention. The latter group is the one that [[Eddan Voss|Eddan]] finds worth watching.

Eddan has at least two people in Greyveil whose primary function is observing this community and reporting anything that seems significant. He has not yet decided what significant would look like, which is part of why the watchers have not been given instructions beyond "notice things."

## Character

Greyveil itself is a frontier city in the practical sense: resourceful, self-reliant, and accustomed to being the last place that has something before the map runs out. It has a trade culture built on serving travelers going further north and coming back from further north, and the result is a city with a higher-than-average tolerance for unusual people, unusual goods, and unusual information. Greyveil has been fielding questions about Wolfpoint for several generations. Most locals have a working account of what it is, most of which is partially accurate.

## Relay Operations

Greyveil's practical leverage in northern logistics is the [[Greyveil-Wolfpoint Relay Track]]. Dispatch houses here handle packet intake, copy protocols, and relay scheduling for correspondence and compact high-value goods moving to and from [[Wolfpoint]].

Because route conditions are variable, Greyveil offices plan by arrival bands rather than exact timestamps, which has become a local administrative specialty.
